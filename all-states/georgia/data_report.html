6. Violations & Inspection Data

Primary sources

  • DECAL Enforcement Actions tracker: EnforcementActions/Default.aspx with type=1 (Denials), type=2 (Revocations/Refusals), type=3 (Fines/Restrictions).
  • Compliance at a Glance dashboard: per-provider compliance zones (Good Standing / Support / Deficient) with 12-month visit history.
  • Visit Report PDFs: linked from provider detail pages (pattern: /Provider/PrintVisitReport.aspx?VisitID={id}).

Data format

ASP.NET portal with "Save results to File" CSV export on Enforcement pages. Inspection types (per CCS-1700): Licensing Study (annual full), Monitoring Visit (annual partial), Complaint / Incident Investigation (reactive). Compliance zones: Good Standing, Support, Deficient.

Freshness

  • Provider Data Export: nightly refresh
  • Enforcement list: 30–60 day lag from initial adverse action to posting
  • Compliance at a Glance: rolling 12-month window
  • Visit Report PDFs: posted within 14 days of visit closure

Scraping / access strategy

  • Enforcement CSVs — union of type=1..4 via Save-results-to-File (trivial ASP.NET postback).
  • Provider detail + Compliance at a Glance: iterate Facility IDs from the Provider Data Export CSV; fetch /ChildCare/Details/{FacilityId}.
  • Visit Report PDFs: discover via detail pages; archive for OCR.
  • No CAPTCHA; basic session cookie required.

FOIA path

Georgia Open Records Act (O.C.G.A. § 50-18-70 et seq.). Submit to decal.openrecords@decal.ga.gov; 3-business-day statutory response. DECAL Open Records form. Useful for un-redacted complaint files and deficiency statements (CCS-1800).

7. Outreach Signals

  • Compliance zone = "Deficient" on Compliance at a Glance (available via per-provider scrape) — highest intent.
  • Fine or Restricted License issued in last 90 days (available in type=3 CSV export).
  • Revocation / Refusal to Renew pending (available in type=2 CSV).
  • License expiration within 90 days (available — annual renewal cycle, end-date in Provider Data Export).
  • Quality Rated star rating = 0 (not yet rated) or recently downgraded (available in Provider Data Export).
