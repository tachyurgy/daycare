# Tennessee — Child Care Licensing Compliance Reference

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** Tennessee Department of Human Services (TDHS), Child Care Licensing program. (Child care operated by public schools is licensed by the Tennessee Department of Education / TDOE; TDHS has primary jurisdiction over private, nonprofit, and for-profit providers.)
- **URL:** https://www.tn.gov/humanservices/for-families/child-care-services.html
- **Provider eLicensing portal:** https://tdhs.service-now.com/tncc
- **Public locator:** https://www.tn.gov/humanservices/for-families/child-care-services/find-child-care.html
- **Complaint hotline:** 1-800-462-8261

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **Governing statute:** Tennessee Child Care Licensing Act of 2000, T.C.A. § 71-3-501 et seq.
- **Administrative rules:** Tenn. Comp. R. & Regs. **Chapter 1240-04-01** — Licensure Rules for Child Care Agencies (Centers, Family & Group Homes, Drop-In Centers). Latest revised version **November 2025** (effective Nov 20, 2025).

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Child Care Center** — 13+ children for 3+ hours/day (1240-04-01-.22).
- **Family Child Care Home** — 5–7 children in provider's residence (1240-04-01-.20).
- **Group Child Care Home** — 8–12 children in a residence/non-residence (1240-04-01-.21).
- **Drop-In Child Care Center** — intermittent care, <14 hrs/week per child (1240-04-01-.23).
- License-exempt: summer camps ≤6 weeks; programs operated by a public school system (governed by TDOE under T.C.A. § 49-1-1101); informal/relative care; tribal.

## Key Licensing Forms (TDHS)
- **HS-2660** — Application for Child Care Agency License.
- **HS-3035** — Child's Record / Enrollment Form.
- **HS-3041** — Immunization Certificate (official TN Certificate of Immunization).
- **HS-0166** — Personnel Information Form.
- **HS-2985** — Criminal Background Check Request.
- **HS-0222** — Serious Incident / Injury Report (within 24 hrs for hospitalization, death, or abuse allegation).
- **HS-3033** — Medication Administration Log / Authorization.
- **HS-3038** — Emergency Management Plan.
- **HS-0270** — Parent Handbook Acknowledgment.
- **Star-Quality Application** (voluntary QRIS, but required for higher subsidy rates).

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (1240-04-01-.22, Chart 1)
| Age band | Ratio | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Infants (6 weeks – 15 months) | 1:4 | 8 |
| Young Toddlers (12 – 30 months) | 1:6 | 12 |
| Older Toddlers (24 – 35 months) | 1:7 | 14 |
| 3-year-olds | 1:9 | 18 |
| 4-year-olds | 1:13 | 20 |
| 5-year-olds | 1:16 | 20 |
| School-age | 1:20 | — |

Centers may elect Chart 2 (mixed toddler grouping) or Chart 3 (first/last 90 minutes only).
**Infant and toddler groups may NEVER exceed ratio or group size** — the 10%-variance allowance (3 days/week) does NOT apply to infants/toddlers.
Second adult required on premises whenever **more than 12 children** are present.
Food preparers are not counted in ratio while preparing meals.

**Family Home (.20):** 1 provider, max 7 children incl. own under age 5.
**Group Home (.21):** 2 adults, max 12 children total.

## Required Child Records
- Enrollment record (HS-3035) with emergency contact, authorized pickups, parent signature.
- **Immunization Certificate** (HS-3041) — official Tennessee form only; medical/religious exemptions documented per T.C.A. § 49-6-5001.
- Physical exam within 30 days of enrollment (some exemptions for religious objection).
- Parental consent for emergency medical treatment, field trips, transportation, water activities.
- Allergy/special-needs care plan.
- Daily attendance sign-in/out signed by parent.
- Medication administration log.
- Incident/injury reports (copies provided to parents same day).
- Records retained **minimum 1 year after child disenrolls**.

## Required Staff Records
- **Background checks:** fingerprint-based TBI and FBI criminal history; TN Sex Offender Registry; TN Felony Offender Database; Abuse Registry (DCS Central Registry); out-of-state child abuse registry checks if resided out-of-state prior 5 years. Required **before unsupervised contact** and refreshed **every 5 years** (federal CCDBG).
- **Personnel file per 1240-04-01-.10:** application, qualifications (education/experience), annual performance evaluation, training records, signed policies, TB screening, references.
- **Director qualifications:** CDA, associate's in ECE, or equivalent per .05(5).
- **Annual training:** **18 clock hours per year** for teaching staff (director must document annually); orientation within first month (includes safe sleep, shaken baby, mandated reporter, emergency plan).
- **CPR/First Aid:** current pediatric certification — at least one certified adult present at all times.
- **TB test** at hire.
- **Mandatory Reporter** training annually.

## Inspection Cadence
- **Licensed agencies:** inspected at least **four times per year** — **three unannounced** and **one annual evaluation** for compliance with star-rated quality system (1240-04-01-.06). Programs in the Star-Quality system receive additional quality evaluations.
- **Complaint investigations** any time.
- **Fire inspection** and **Environmental/Sanitation** inspection annually (via TN SFMO and local health dept).
- Inspection reports publicly posted on TDHS provider search and must be posted in a conspicuous place at the facility.

## Renewal Cadence
- **Annual license term** — license expires 12 months from issuance and must be renewed each year (1240-04-01-.04(3)).
- Temporary approval / provisional license (up to 6 months) may be issued while meeting outstanding items.

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Report Card + Star-Quality Rating** (0–3 stars): public rating posted; directly tied to subsidy reimbursement rate. Star evaluations are conducted annually by TDHS evaluators using a standardized instrument.
- **Tennessee Early Childhood Training Alliance (TECTA):** approved PD registry for staff training hours.
- **Annual "facility assessment"** including ERS (Environment Rating Scale) observations for star ratings.
- **Safe Sleep Law (T.C.A. § 68-1-127)** — mandatory safe-sleep training for any staff caring for children under 12 months; written safe-sleep policy posted in every infant room.
- **Vehicle inspections** — any vehicle used for transportation must be inspected and documented; driver must be 21+ with valid CDL (bus) or commercial endorsement for 10+ passengers.
- **Child Care Management System (CCMS)** electronic attendance for Certificate-program (subsidy) children.
- **Serious injury / death** reporting within 24 hours; mandatory written report within 48 hours.
- **Emergency preparedness plan:** includes weather (tornado), active-threat lockdown; monthly fire drills, quarterly severe-weather drills.
- **Abuse/neglect** allegations: agency must report to DCS immediately (T.C.A. § 37-1-403); failure is itself grounds for revocation.
- **Child abuse summary** must be posted at entrance with fiscal-year counts of abuses, injuries, and deaths per TDHS Digital Tennessee publication.
