# Virginia — Child Care Licensing Compliance Reference

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** Virginia Department of Education (VDOE), Division of Early Childhood Care and Education, Office of Child Care Licensing. (Child care licensing was transferred from Virginia Department of Social Services to VDOE effective July 1, 2021 under HB 2299.)
- **URL:** https://www.doe.virginia.gov/teaching-learning-assessment/early-childhood-care-education
- **Provider portal:** https://www.childcare.virginia.gov/providers
- **Public search:** https://dss.virginia.gov/facility/search/cc2.cgi (legacy URL still live) and https://www.childcare.virginia.gov/find-care

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **Standards for Licensed Child Day Centers:** 8VAC20-780 (effective October 13, 2021; amendments effective January 1, 2026; proposed rulemaking 42:8 published Dec 1, 2025).
- **Standards for Licensed Family Day Homes:** 8VAC20-790.
- **Background Checks for Child Day Programs and Family Day Systems:** 8VAC20-770 (new combined rule 8VAC20-821 **effective February 1, 2026**).
- **General Procedures for Licensure:** 8VAC20-820.
- **Statute:** Code of Virginia § 22.1-289.010 et seq. (formerly § 63.2-1700 et seq.).

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Licensed Child Day Center** — 13+ children, or any facility not a residence.
- **Licensed Family Day Home** — 5–12 children in provider's residence (mandatory licensure).
- **Voluntarily Registered Family Day Home** — ≤4 children, optional registration.
- **Certified Preschools** — programs operated by schools accredited by an approved accrediting body.
- **Licensed Family Day System** — sponsor organization for 3+ family day homes.
- **Religiously Exempt Centers** — file Letter of Intent and annual reports; not fully licensed but must meet background-check requirements.
- **Local Ordinance Approved Homes** (Northern Virginia — Fairfax, Arlington, Alexandria, Prince William, Falls Church) — regulated by local ordinance in lieu of state license.
- **School-age recreational/instructional** — partially exempt.

## Key Licensing Forms (VDOE)
- **032-05-0001** — Application for a License to Operate a Child Day Program.
- **032-05-0024** — Injury Report (serious injury within 2 business days).
- **032-05-0075** — Child's Physical Examination Report.
- **032-05-0029** — Sworn Disclosure Statement (background).
- **032-05-0030** — Central Registry Release of Information.
- **OBC-SP-130-081-08** — Fingerprint card / Livescan submission.
- **CPS Background Check Request** — DSS CPS search.
- **Medication Authorization form** (VDOE template).
- **Emergency Preparedness Plan** (written).
- **Self-inspection checklist** (required annually).
- **Child Care PASS** (Provider Attendance Submission System) — launched Dec 1, 2025 for subsidy attendance.

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (8VAC20-780-350)
| Age band | Ratio (staff:children) | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Birth up to 16 months (infant) | 1:4 | 12 |
| 16 months up to 24 months (toddler) | 1:5 | 15 |
| 2 years old | 1:8 | 24 |
| 3 years – school-age eligible | 1:10 | 30 |
| School-age up to 9 years | 1:18 | — |
| 9 through 12 years | 1:20 | — |

Ratio and group size applicable to the **youngest** child apply to the entire mixed-age group.
Rest-period relaxed ratios allowed with staff within sight/sound and backup on-site.
Group-size limits do not apply during outdoor play, meals, transportation, or first/last hour of operation.

**Family Day Homes (8VAC20-790):** provider may care for up to 12 children (inclusive of own children <age school), no more than 4 under 2 yrs; 1:4 ratio for children under 16 months.

## Required Child Records
- Enrollment/application form incl. emergency contacts (8VAC20-780-60).
- **Physical examination report** within 30 days of enrollment (or prior copies from provider up to 12 months old).
- **Immunization record** meeting § 22.1-271.2 (VA School Entry Schedule) — harmonized with Virginia Department of Health schedule; religious/medical exemptions on VDH Form.
- **Allergy and chronic-condition care plan** where applicable.
- Authorized-pickup list.
- Consent to emergency medical treatment.
- Daily attendance.
- **Written care plan** for any child requiring medication, therapy, or specific diet.
- Medication administration log.

## Required Staff Records
- **Background checks (8VAC20-770 / new 8VAC20-821):** fingerprint-based state/FBI criminal history; VA Child Protective Services (CPS) Central Registry search; sex offender registry; out-of-state CPS checks if lived out-of-state in prior 5 years. Refreshed every **5 years**. New combined rule effective **February 1, 2026** — retroactive checks required for staff whose most recent check is older than 5 years.
- **Sworn disclosure statement** prior to hire (self-disclosure of convictions/findings).
- **Education/credential:** Program director must meet qualifications in 8VAC20-780-240; lead program staff per 8VAC20-780-245.
- **Training:** Minimum of **16 hours per year** (8VAC20-780-270) of professional development, of which at least half must be on topics required by CCDBG (health, safety, child development). New staff must complete orientation training covering 11 required topics within 90 days.
- **CPR/First Aid:** Pediatric CPR and first aid required for all staff who count in ratio; at least one certified staff member present at all times.
- **Daily health observation** documented.
- **TB screening** per Department of Health guidance.
- **VDOE-approved Medication Administration Training (MAT)** for any staff administering prescription meds (100-hour curriculum for VDOE MAT-Child Day program trainers; 6–8-hour course for administration).
- **Mandated reporter** training on hire and annually.

## Inspection Cadence
- Announced and unannounced inspections during the license term (8VAC20-820-180). VDOE targets at least **one unannounced inspection per licensed center per year** (industry norm, not codified).
- Renewal inspection prior to license expiration.
- Complaint inspections at any time based on allegation severity.
- Family Day Systems: **at least twice per year**.
- Fire marshal inspection annually.

## Renewal Cadence
- Standard license term: **2 years** (historically; in practice VDOE issues licenses for varying terms — 6 months provisional, 1-year conditional, 2-year standard, 3-year for demonstrated compliance history).
- License must be posted on premises with expiration date.
- Provisional (up to 6 months) may be issued while applicant completes requirements; conditional (up to 1 year) where compliance is intermittent.

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Child Care PASS** (Provider Attendance Submission System) — launched December 1, 2025 for subsidy providers; replaces prior attendance system.
- **VQB5** — Virginia Quality Birth to Five measurement & improvement system; mandatory for publicly-funded ECE classrooms, optional for purely private pay.
- **Mixed-Delivery / VPI+ Grant** — state-funded pre-K programs; participating centers have additional curriculum and ratio requirements.
- **Serious injury reporting** within 2 business days; **death/loss** within 24 hrs.
- **Sudden and Unexpected Death in Infants (SUID)** policy required.
- **Emergency preparedness plan** with annual practice drill documentation (fire monthly; evac/lockdown/shelter in place: 2 each per year).
- **Playground safety** — compliant with CPSC public playground guidelines; annual inspection documented.
- **Safe-sleep** policy mandatory; infant sleep in cribs meeting CPSC; no inclined sleepers.
- **Family Day Home** providers must disclose if animals are on premises; any children in care are included in their ratio.
