## A Quick Note for Parents and Guardians

Your child's daycare asked you to upload some documents (usually immunization records, emergency contact forms, or signed authorizations) using **ComplianceKit**. Before you upload, here is what you should know in plain language. This takes about 60 seconds to read.

### Who we are

ComplianceKit is the software tool your daycare uses to keep child records organized and in line with state regulations. We are a separate company from your daycare. Your daycare chose us because paper files and spreadsheets make it hard to keep track of immunization deadlines, forms, and signatures.

### What your daycare asked you to upload

Your daycare will tell you exactly which documents they need. Typical examples:

- Immunization records
- Emergency contact or pickup authorization forms
- Allergy or medical action plans
- Signed parent handbook acknowledgments

You should only upload documents your daycare asked for. Please do not upload Social Security cards, birth certificates with full Social Security numbers, bank information, or anything else your daycare did not request.

### Who can see what you upload

- **Your daycare.** They are the ones who asked for the documents and they control who at the daycare has access.
- **ComplianceKit** (us). We store the file securely and help your daycare stay organized. We act on your daycare's instructions.
- **A small set of trusted vendors** we use to run the service (for example, to host the file, send notifications, or read the text on a scanned document). A current list is at our Sub-processors page.

We do **not** sell your information. We do **not** use it for advertising. We do **not** share it with anyone else unless the law requires us to.

### About photos from your phone

If you upload a photo, our system will automatically read the text on it (for example, to pull out the vaccine name and date). This is called OCR. The photo and the text we pull from it are stored for your daycare.

We will **not** use the contents of your photos for anything other than helping your daycare track the records they asked for.

### How long we keep it

We keep your child's documents while your child is enrolled at the daycare, and for up to 90 days after your child leaves (in case the daycare needs to fix a record or respond to an inspector). After that, the documents are deleted.

Some daycare records must be kept longer to follow state law. In those cases, your daycare decides, not us.

### Your rights

You can ask for:

- A copy of what we have about your child.
- To fix anything that is wrong.
- To delete information (subject to laws that may require your daycare to keep some records).

The easiest way is to ask your daycare — they are in charge of the records and have the tools to help you right away. If you need our help, email us at {{LEGAL_EMAIL}}.

### Your child's safety

We use strong encryption, limit who at ComplianceKit can see your files, and follow industry-standard security practices. If anything goes wrong, we will tell your daycare within 72 hours and they will tell you.

### Questions?

Reply to your daycare's message first — they are the fastest way to get an answer. For anything ComplianceKit-specific, email {{LEGAL_EMAIL}}.
