# Mississippi — Child Care Compliance Overview

## Licensing Authority

**Mississippi State Department of Health (MSDH) — Child Care Facilities Licensure Branch**
- Address: 143 Lefleur's Square, Jackson, MS 39216
- Phone: 1-800-227-7308 / 601-364-2827
- Web: https://msdh.ms.gov/page/30,0,183.html

## Primary Regulatory Framework

- **Statute:** Mississippi Code Ann. §43-20-1 et seq. ("Mississippi Child Care Licensing Law"), authorizing MSDH to prescribe minimum regulations for child care facilities.
- **Regulations:** Title 15 Miss. Admin. Code, Part 11 (Bureau of Child Care Facilities), Subpart 55 (Child Care Facilities Licensure).
  - **Chapter 1:** Regulations Governing Licensure of Child Care Facilities (centers)
  - **Chapter 2:** Regulations Governing Licensure of Child Care Facilities for 12 or Fewer Children in the Operator's Home
- Authoritative citation throughout is "Miss. Code Ann. §43-20-8" (MSDH rulemaking authority).

## Facility Types Regulated

- Child care **Center** (13+ children), regulated under Chapter 1.
- Child care in **operator's home with 12 or fewer children**, regulated under Chapter 2.
- **Drop-in facilities**, **group homes**, and **school-age-only programs** fall under the same licensure scheme with age-specific adjustments.
- Family child care providers serving ≤ 5 unrelated children in the provider's home are exempt from licensure (voluntary registration option).

## Key Licensing Forms

- **MSDH Child Care Facility Inspection Report — Form #281**
- **MSDH Food Service Inspection — Forms #301/#302**
- **MSDH Fire Inspection — Form #333** (must be signed by local fire inspection authority)
- **Letter of Suitability for Employment** issued by MSDH (background check clearance)
- **Initial/Renewal License Application** (submitted online via MSDH portal)

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (Rule 1.8.2, 1.8.3)

| Age of Children | Staff : Children | Max Group Size | Min Sq. Ft. per Child |
|---|---|---|---|
| Infants (birth – 12 months) | 1 : 5 | 10 | 40 |
| Toddlers (12 – 24 months) | 1 : 9 | 18 | 45 |
| 2 years | 1 : 12 | 24 | 35 |
| 3 years | 1 : 14 | 28 | 35 |
| 4 years | 1 : 16 | 32 | 35 |
| 5 – 9 years | 1 : 20 | 40 | 35 |
| 10 – 12 years | 1 : 25 | 50 | 35 |

Ratios must be met **at all times**, including arrival/departure, field trips, and water activities. **In mixed-age groups, the youngest child's ratio governs.** Preschool children may not be combined with school-age children during normal programming.

## Required Child Records (Rule 1.6 series)

- Enrollment form (name, address, DOB, emergency contacts)
- Immunization certificate (Form 121 equivalent) or medical/religious exemption
- Health examination / medical history
- Allergies, special dietary needs, medication authorizations
- Authorized pick-up list
- Parent/guardian handbook acknowledgment
- Daily attendance record (arrival/departure times, signed by parent or staff)
- Records retained for the child's period of enrollment

## Required Staff Records (Subchapter 5)

- Application & employment history
- **FBI/MCIC fingerprint criminal history check** + child abuse central registry + sex offender registry (processed through MSDH Records Check Unit)
- **Letter of Suitability for Employment** issued by MSDH
- Current TB screening
- Documentation of orientation (within 1 month of hire)
- Adult, Child, & Infant CPR and First Aid certification (required for staff on premises at all times)
- ServSafe© / TummySafe© / National Registry food manager certificate (if applicable)
- Annual training log (minimum continuing ed hours)
- Date of employment & separation; exit information

## Inspection Cadence

- **Pre-licensure & pre-renewal** inspections by MSDH agency representative (Rule 1.2.5).
- Facilities are **subject to unannounced inspection at any time** at MSDH discretion.
- Findings recorded on MSDH Form #281 and left with the operator at the time of inspection.
- Additional required inspections prior to licensure: fire (Form #333), food service (Forms #301/#302), and sanitation.

## Renewal Cadence

- Licenses are issued for **one year** and may be renewed annually (Rule 1.2.7).
- MSDH emails renewal notice ≥ 75 days before expiration.
- Renewal application + fee + current inspection certificates due **≥ 30 days before expiration**.
- Late filing incurs a **$25 late fee**.
- Lapsed licenses may be reinstated within one month of expiration by paying renewal + reinstatement fees; after that, a new initial application is required.
- Licenses are **not transferable**; new operator or new location requires a new license.

## Notable State-Specific Requirements

- **Mixed-age grouping rule:** youngest child determines ratio; preschool and school-age children may not be combined during classroom or water activities.
- **Rest period exception:** for children ≥ 2 years, facility may operate at 50% of required staff in the same room during rest periods, provided the overall staff:child ratio is maintained on-premises; children < 2 must always have full ratio in-room.
- **Food safety:** served milk must be fat-free/1% for children ≥ 2 years; whole milk for children < 2. Raw vegetables may not be served to children under 2 (choking hazard).
- **Sun safety programming** must be incorporated into activities for all age levels (Rule 1.9.1).
- Director and all caregivers must pass MSDH fingerprint-based background check + state/federal registry checks; check must be re-run at least every 5 years.
- **Food Manager Certification** required for facility with on-site food preparation (TummySafe©, ServSafe©, or National Registry).
- Compliance with group sizes not required during normal arrival/departure or special events, but age-appropriate **staff:child ratio must still be maintained**.
- **SIDS / safe sleep:** infants must be placed on backs on firm surface; doctor's order required to allow stomach-sleeping or dietary exceptions (Rules 1.9.1 & 1.10.7).
