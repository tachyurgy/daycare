6.Violations & Inspection Data

LDE publishes per-facility inspection history directly on louisianaschools.com. Each facility has an /Inspections page listing every visit (initial, annual monitoring, complaint, critical-incident follow-up) with all 43 Bulletin 137 regulations flagged in- or out-of-compliance. Records remain public indefinitely.

Full details: violations.md

7.Outreach Signals

Regulatory pain points

  • CCCBC (Child Care Criminal Background Check) — one of the most comprehensive in the U.S.: state + FBI + SOR + state CA/N + out-of-state checks for recent residents.
  • Orientation training covering 15 topic areas within 7 calendar days of hire.
  • Type III centers face additional Bulletin 140 academic-performance standards.
  • Emergency Preparedness Plan must include hurricane evacuation, shelter-in-place, lockdown, and reunification protocols.
  • Critical Incident Reports within 24 hours — missed reports are a common citation.

Market heat & channel

  • Louisiana publicly revokes licenses — news releases create a visible enforcement rhythm.
  • 65 Early Childhood Community Networks coordinate publicly-funded seats — a channel to reach Type III centers at scale.
  • LA Quality Start "Performance Profile" (Bulletin 140) is the QRIS lever — star rating upgrades drive software-purchase budget.
  • LA Pathways workforce registry is another integration target.
