6.Violations & Inspection Data

Kentucky publishes inspection reports and "non-compliance" citations per facility on the kynect Child Care Provider Details page. Reports include plans of correction and All STARS rating history. The kynect front-end is a Salesforce Lightning Web Component — full bulk access requires a headless browser or a KRS 61 open-records request.

Full details: violations.md

  • CHFS Inspection & Abuse/Injury Data landing — chfs.ky.gov — inspection & abuse/injury
  • Annual aggregate Injury/Abuse Report (PDF, refreshed each year) linked on the CHFS page.
  • KRS 61.872 requires response to open-records requests within 5 business days.

7.Outreach Signals

Regulatory pain points

  • CCBC 5-component background check must clear before any new hire starts.
  • 15 hours annual in-service training per staff; 6 hours for CFCCH.
  • Transportation log retention is 5 years — unusually long; a software advantage.
  • TB screening every 2 years for all staff.
  • Serious Incident Reports within 24 hours.
  • Quarterly inspections during 2-year probation after a cited violation.

Market heat & channel

  • Kentucky All STARS mandatory for all licensed centers — Level 3-5 upgrades drive software budget.
  • Louisville, Lexington, N. Kentucky metro, Bowling Green, Owensboro = priority MSAs.
  • Child Care Aware of Kentucky referrals ((877) 316-3552) — high-intent directory for parents.
  • CHFS publishes a Consumer Education Statement highlighting the kynect portal — parents reference it, driving director compliance urgency.

8.Next-Step Data Acquisition

  1. File a Kentucky Open Records request (chfsoigrccportal@ky.gov) for the full licensee roster + 24-month non-compliance history in CSV/Excel. 5-day response requirement per KRS 61.872.
  2. Build Playwright-based scraper against the kynect Salesforce portal to harvest provider record IDs; use residential proxies and rate-limit aggressively.
  3. Once record IDs are seeded, fetch /benefits/s/child-care-provider-details?id={id} and parse inspection + citation table; download plan-of-correction PDFs.
  4. Enrich phones / emails via commercial enrichment against matched Type I / II centers.
  5. Cross-reference Kentucky All STARS rating levels for lead scoring — recent "non-compliance" citations + non-5-star rating = highest-intent target.
