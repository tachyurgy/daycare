6. Violations & Inspection Data new

Child Care Check is DCYF's Salesforce-hosted public database where each licensed provider has a detail page listing current status, valid complaints (DCYF-verified — unfounded complaints are not shown), monitoring visit outcomes, and enforcement actions.

Freshness

  • Valid complaints post after DCYF investigation closes (45-day median).
  • Monitoring reports post ~2–3 weeks post-visit.
  • Roster (Socrata was8-3ni8): near-real-time / monthly.

Access Strategy

  • Apex Remoting: load PSS_Search?... to capture session + CSRF tokens, then POST against /apexremote with the PSS_SearchController.queryProviders action for bulk JSON. Follow with per-facility PSS_Provider?id=<sf_id> loads for inspection-document URLs.
  • Playwright fallback for non-scriptable paths. Salesforce Sites tolerates ~1 req/sec per worker.
  • Socrata roster join: CSV download at data.wa.gov/api/views/was8-3ni8/rows.csv?accessType=DOWNLOAD gives identifiers; cross-walk via FamLinkId / SSPSProviderNumber against Salesforce account IDs.
PRA backup. Washington Public Records Act (RCW 42.56) — 5-business-day initial response required. DCYF portal: dcyf.wa.gov/practice/public-records. Use for structured CSV of licensing investigations, summary suspensions, revocations, denials, and non-renewal orders scoped by date range.

7. Outreach Signals (Hot-Lead Detection)

  • Probationary license on Child Care Check — immediate compliance fix required; highest-intent.
  • Facility Licensing Compliance Agreement (FLCA) in effect — DCYF has formally identified a pattern; provider is working against a deadline.
  • Valid complaint in past 12 months — post-incident operators are the most receptive buyers.
  • Early Achievers rating below Level 3 — subsidy reimbursement gated above Level 3; providers below are losing revenue.
  • License expiration in next 90 days (computed from LicenseExpirationDate in Socrata) — renewal inspection is the year's biggest event.
  • Outdoor Nature-Based Programs (WA-unique) — small sub-segment with unique compliance checklist; niche but high-fit.
