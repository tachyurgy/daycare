# Nevada — Child Care Licensing Compliance Reference

_Compiled 2026-04-18 for ComplianceKit. All citations are to publicly-available primary sources — verify before relying in production._

## Licensing Authority

- **State of Nevada — Division of Supportive Services (DSS), Child Care Licensing Program (CCL)**
  - Consolidated 7/1/2024 (previously split between DPBH and DWSS); now statewide single licensing authority.
- Statewide portal: https://www.dpbh.nv.gov/regulatory/child-care-facilities/
- Find-a-facility: https://findchildcare.nv.gov (search portal powered by Aithent)
- Clark County and Washoe County have **local licensing partners** for family child care homes (separate county regs):
  - Clark County Business License / Social Service
  - Washoe County Social Services — Family Child Care Home licensing
- Nevada Registry (professional development): https://www.nevadaregistry.org

## Primary Regulatory Framework

- **Nevada Revised Statutes (NRS) Chapter 432A** — Services and Facilities for Care of Children
- **Nevada Administrative Code (NAC) Chapter 432A** — implementing regulations
  - NAC 432A.5205 — Minimum staffing ratios
  - NAC 432A.190 — Inspections
  - NAC 432A.300s — training & qualifications
- **NRS 432A.1773–432A.1775** — licensee registration and training requirements

## Facility Types Regulated

| Type | Scope |
|------|-------|
| Child Care Center | Non-residential, ≥5 children |
| Child Care Institution | Residential care of children |
| Nursery for Infants & Toddlers | Specialized infant/toddler |
| Special Needs Facility | Serves children with disabilities |
| Accommodation Facility | Hotel/casino drop-in (Kids Quest-style) |
| Facility for Special Events | Short-term event-based |
| Outdoor Youth Program | Camps / wilderness programs |
| Family Child Care Home | In-residence (county-licensed in Clark/Washoe) |
| Group Child Care Home | Small group in residence |

Night-care regulations also apply (NAC 432A.5205 — staff on duty at night must remain awake).

## Key Licensing Forms (DPBH/DSS CCL)

- **CCL-001** — Application for License
- **CCL-002** — Facility Information / Floor Plan Form
- **CCL-100** — Staff Roster & Background Check Attestation
- **NRS 432A.170 Background Check Authorization** — Nevada DPS Records & ID Services + FBI fingerprint + CANS (child abuse/neglect screen) + sex-offender
- **Emergency / Disaster Plan Form**
- **Medication Administration Authorization Form**
- **Injury/Incident Report**
- **Immunization Record** per Nevada Division of Public Health schedule
- **Director Qualifications Form**

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (NAC 432A.5205)

Daytime (6:30 AM – 9:00 PM):

| Age Band | Ratio | Max Group |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| Infant (0–12 mo) | 1:4 | 12 (up to 3 caregivers) |
| Toddler (1–2 yr) | 1:6 | 12 |
| 2-year-olds | 1:8 | 16 |
| 3-year-olds | 1:10 | 20 |
| 4-year-olds | 1:13 | 26 |
| 5-year-olds | 1:13 | 26 |
| School-age | 1:15 | 30 |

Night (9:00 PM – 6:30 AM): different reduced ratios; all night staff **must remain awake**.

**Mixed-age groups:** ratio/group size based on **youngest child**.

**Washoe County Home Providers**: local caps differ; verify with Washoe County Social Services.

## Required Child Records

- Enrollment & emergency form
- **Immunization certificate** — Nevada Division of Public Health (or exemption)
- Physical / health statement
- Authorization: medical, pickup, field trip, photography, diaper cream
- Allergy / special-needs plans
- Medication authorization
- Daily attendance (sign in/out)
- Incident / injury log
- Food allergy / diet plan

## Required Staff Records

- Application / references
- **Nevada DPS Records & ID Services** criminal background check + **FBI fingerprint** + **Central Registry** (child abuse) + sex-offender registry — BEFORE unsupervised contact
- Health statement (TB per Nevada DPBH)
- **Nevada Registry** enrollment (career ladder for professional development)
- Pre-service orientation
- **Annual training — 24 hours** (NRS 432A.1775) for each person employed in child care facility, other than sick-child facility
- CPR + Pediatric First Aid current
- Infant safe sleep / SIDS training for infant rooms
- Medication administration training
- Mandated reporter acknowledgment (NRS 432B.220)

## Inspection Cadence

- **At least 2 inspections per 12-month licensing period (every 6 months); unannounced** (NRS 432A.180 / NAC 432A.190)
- Complaint + follow-up as needed
- Inspection results posted publicly on findchildcare.nv.gov

## Renewal Cadence

- **Annual license renewal** (12-month term)
- Tied to background-check currency, annual training compliance, inspection results
- Fees vary by capacity

## Notable State-Specific Requirements

- **24-hour annual training** requirement for all caregivers (NRS 432A.1775) — higher than most states.
- **Awake staff at night** — night-care facilities must have staff awake on duty (NAC 432A.5205).
- **Nevada Registry** professional development — enrollment mandatory for licensed staff.
- **Silver State Stars QRIS** — Nevada's voluntary quality rating system.
- **Background checks via DPS + FBI + CANS + sex-offender registry** — note statutory exclusion list.
- **Clark County / Washoe County family homes** fall under **county licensing** — state licensing largely covers centers + southern/northern county centers.
- **Emergency drills** — fire monthly; earthquake/shelter-in-place + evacuation required annually.
- **Air-quality / heat advisories** — outdoor play guidance during smoke and heat events (Western wildfire / desert climate).
- **Water safety** — strict rules for pools, fountains, and field trips near open water.
- **Transportation** — specific rules for seat restraints and vehicle safety checklists.
