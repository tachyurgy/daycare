7. Outreach Signals

  • 2-year license cycle with 6–18-month mandatory inspection window — predictable inspection triggers.
  • Mandatory 60-min daily outdoor play (even in winter) — Maine-specific documentation burden.
  • 30 hrs/yr training for center lead staff — above federal floor; certification-tracking pain.
  • Rising Stars QRIS is voluntary but linked to CCAP subsidy tiers; publicly rated.
  • Widely-reported enforcement gap creates headline risk every time a facility's inspection history goes public — parents-as-auditors dynamic.
  • Failure-to-report enforcement wave (2024-2025) — 60 providers cited for failing to notify state or parents of incidents. Incident-logging discipline is a current buying trigger.
