# North Carolina — Child Care Compliance Reference

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** North Carolina Department of Health and Human Services (NCDHHS)
- **Division:** Division of Child Development and Early Education (DCDEE)
- **URL:** https://ncchildcare.ncdhhs.gov/
- Office location (as of October 2025): 1915 Health Services Way, Raleigh, NC 27607
- Contact: (919) 814-6300 or (800) 859-0829 (in-state)

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **N.C. Gen. Stat. Chapter 110, Article 7** — Child Care enabling statute ("Child Care Facilities Act")
- **10A N.C. Admin. Code, Chapter 09** — Child Care Rules (the operating rulebook)
- **10A NCAC 09 .0102** — Definitions
- **10A NCAC 09 .0302** — Application for a License for a Child Care Center
- **10A NCAC 09 .0713** — Staff/Child Ratios for Centers
- **10A NCAC 09 .2818** — Enhanced Staff/Child Ratios for a Rated License
- **10A NCAC 09 .2703** — Qualifications & Background Checks
- **10A NCAC 09 .2800 series** — Star Rated License System (NC's QRIS is built into licensing)

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Licensed Child Care Center** — 3+ pre-school children or 9+ school-agers, non-residential
- **Licensed Family Child Care Home (FCCH)** — up to 8 children in operator's residence (5 preschoolers including operator's own, plus up to 3 school-agers)
- **Religious-sponsored programs** — subject to Chapter 110, Article 7, Part 2 (reduced requirements)
- **Non-licensed / exempt** programs (short-duration, recreational) — separate path

## Key Licensing Forms
- **DCDEE Child Care Facility License Application** (online via eLicensing system)
- **CJS 150 / DCDEE-0280** — Qualifications Letter Application
- **DCDEE-0281** — Staff Application / Background Check Package
- **Criminal Background Check (CBC)** — fingerprint-based via NC SBI / FBI (submitted through NCABCMS at https://ncabcms.nc.gov/DCDEE/Applicant/)
- **Child Care Medical Action Plan**
- **Emergency Information Form** for each child
- **DCDEE-3111** — Workforce Education Information form
- **Form DEHNR 3987** (Food Service Sanitation) — for centers preparing food
- **Local fire inspection certificate** (annual)
- **Local building/sanitation inspection certificate**
- **Health Assessment** for staff

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (10A NCAC 09 .0713)
| Age band (single-age group) | Ratio | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Infants (0–12 months) | 1:5 | 10 |
| Toddlers (12–24 months) | 1:6 | 12 |
| 2–3 years | 1:10 | 20 |
| 3–4 years | 1:15 | 25 |
| 4–5 years | 1:20 | 25 |
| School-age (5+ years) | 1:25 | 25 |

- Combined-age groups use ratio for the **youngest** child in the group
- Infants under 1 year must generally be kept separate from 2+
- **Multi-age groupings** (e.g., 1:4 for 0–36 months) permitted with written notice to the Division
- **10A NCAC 09 .2818 Enhanced Ratios** — required for 4- and 5-star licenses (tighter than base)

## Required Child Records
- Enrollment application with parent/guardian, emergency contacts (2+), authorized pick-up list
- **Immunization record** per N.C. Immunization Rules (DTaP, polio, MMR, Hib, hep B, varicella, PCV, flu)
- Medical/health assessment completed within 30 days of enrollment (physician-signed)
- Special-needs / chronic-condition care plan
- Allergy action plans (with photo if applicable)
- Permission for transportation, field trips, water activities, photography
- Daily attendance record
- Incident / accident reports
- Medication administration records with parent authorization
- Developmental screening documentation (when provided)

## Required Staff Records (10A NCAC 09 .2703 et seq.)
- **Criminal Background Check (CBC)** — fingerprint-based through NC SBI and FBI
- **State central registry** check (Responsible Individuals List — NC child abuse registry)
- **Sex offender registry** check
- **Re-check every 3 years** (rolling as of current NC rules)
- **NC Early Educator Certification** — required level depends on role (Lead Teacher: EEC 5+; Director: EEC tier matching center size)
- Staff health assessment / TB screening at hire
- **BSAC (Basic School-Age Care) or equivalent** for school-age-only staff
- **CPR and first aid** certification (including pediatric)
- **Prevention of shaken baby syndrome / abusive head trauma** — annually
- **Emergency preparedness and response** training
- **ITS-SIDS (Infant/Toddler Safe Sleep and SIDS Risk Reduction)** — annual for infant/toddler room staff
- **Child abuse recognition and reporting** training
- **Minimum annual in-service training** hours (varies by role: 5 hours for administrators above the 3-star threshold; 15+ for staff)
- **NC Early Educator Support, Licensure and Professional Development (EESLPD)** unit registration

## Inspection Cadence
- **Unannounced inspections at any time** — DCDEE may visit to verify ongoing compliance
- **Annual compliance visit** — at minimum
- **Fire safety inspection** — annual (local or state fire marshal)
- **Sanitation/health inspection** — annual (local health department)
- **Star Rating Assessment** — every 3 years (re-rating for 4–5 star centers using ERS + Education Points)
- **Complaint investigation** — triggered by any complaint

## Renewal Cadence
- **Star-Rated License** — valid **3 years** (for 3-star and above rated centers; star rating is re-assessed at least every 3 years)
- 1- and 2-star licenses and Temporary/Provisional licenses: shorter terms with increased monitoring
- Annual license fee required

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Star Rated License System** — NC's QRIS is **mandatory** and built into the license (1–5 stars visible on the license itself). Every licensed center has a star rating based on Program Standards + Education Standards points.
- **NC Foundations for Early Learning and Development** — state early learning standards
- **More at Four / NC Pre-K** participation — additional contractual requirements
- **Responsible Individuals List (RIL)** — NC-specific child abuse registry check is mandatory
- **ITS-SIDS** training required state-wide for infant/toddler classrooms
- **Smart Start / NC Partnership for Children** — regional quality supports, consultation
- **NC ABC Management System (NCABCMS)** — state's background-check platform at https://ncabcms.nc.gov/DCDEE/Applicant/
- **Child Care Health Consultant (CCHC)** — centers with 6+ children must contract with a CCHC (RN/RD/MD) who conducts onsite reviews
- **Temperature, water-activity, and nap-supervision** rules tighter than federal CCDBG floor
- **Emergency Preparedness and Response Plan** required, reviewed annually
- **Star-Rated License requires ERS** (Environment Rating Scales — ITERS-3, ECERS-3, FCCERS-3, SACERS-U)
