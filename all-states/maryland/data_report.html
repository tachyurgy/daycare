Maryland is the strongest state for public inspection transparency in our target set — CheckCCMD has published every inspection since Jan 2011. Typical lag: 30 days from inspection. No API or CSV — bulk violation harvest requires scraping ProviderInformation.aspx?ProvNum=<id> pages. PIA path: MSDE PIA; 30-day standard response, first 2 hours free.

07 Outreach Signals

  • Inspection transparency = competitive pressure. Because every violation is public on CheckCCMD, directors face real reputational risk from even minor lapses — a strong "sleep-at-night" sales narrative.
  • 13 Regional Offices = variable enforcement. Directors in Prince George's / Baltimore City experience heavier scrutiny than Western MD; geo-segmented outreach resonates.
  • Maryland EXCELS Level 3+ is subsidy-gated. Programs chasing tiered reimbursement need a structured document vault — natural upsell.
  • CPDR staff registry is MSDE-mandatory; ComplianceKit staff-file sync to CPDR could be a wedge feature.
  • MFN LOCATE provides a second discovery channel for marketing — programs listed there expect parents to comparison-shop.
