6. Violations & Inspection Data

Primary sources

  • NC DCDEE Child Care Facility Search: ncchildcare.ncdhhs.gov/childcaresearch — per-facility "DCDEE Visits" tab.
  • Legacy Visits.asp: ncchildcaresearch.dhhs.state.nc.us/Visits.asp?Facility_ID={id} — direct URL pattern for per-facility visits (reachable without Telerik UI).
  • Statistical Detail monthly PDF: Jan 2026 edition (813 pages) — full facility list + Administrative Actions section.
  • Find Monitoring Reports how-to: How-Do-I/Find-Monitoring-Reports.

Data format

Two parallel portals: the modern Telerik-powered childcaresearch (encrypted ViewState; requires headless browser) and the legacy Visits.asp Classic ASP page (scrapable with requests+BeautifulSoup). No JSON/CSV API, no Socrata dataset. Administrative actions include Written Warning, Special Provisional, Summary Suspension, License Revocation, Abuse/Neglect findings.

Freshness

  • Visits.asp: 14–30 day lag after inspection close
  • Statistical Detail: monthly (posted first week)
  • Administrative Actions: 60+ day lag for revocations pending legal review

Scraping / access strategy

  • Step 1 (Facility IDs): either OCR the Statistical Detail PDF (text-layered, ~4,500 IDs) or drive the Telerik search with Playwright county-by-county.
  • Step 2 (violations): for each ID, GET Visits.asp?Facility_ID={id}; parse HTML rows of (visit_date, type, reason, violation_Y/N, detail_link). Rate-limit 1 req/sec.
  • Step 3 (admin actions): parse the monthly Statistical Detail's Administrative Actions section.
  • Best ROI: a public-records request asking DCDEE for the full Facility ID list + Administrative Actions CSV — bypasses the Telerik portal entirely.

FOIA path

N.C. Gen. Stat. § 132 (Public Records Act; strong presumption of disclosure). Email publicinfo@dhhs.nc.gov or call 919-814-6300. No statutory deadline; practical turnaround 2–4 weeks. DCDEE has granted Administrative Actions CSVs and Facility ID lists on request.

7. Outreach Signals

  • Visits.asp row in last 90 days with Violations = Yes (requires per-facility scrape; strongest intent).
  • Facility listed in monthly Admin Actions section (available via PDF OCR).
  • Star Rating = 1 or 2 (available — visible on license) — secondary signal.
  • Temporary / Provisional license in effect (available — visible in Facility Search; indicates non-compliance).
  • License expiring within 90 days (available — 3-year term for 3+ star licenses).
