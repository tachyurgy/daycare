# Pennsylvania — Child Care Compliance Reference

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** Pennsylvania Department of Human Services (DHS)
- **Division:** Office of Child Development and Early Learning (OCDEL), Bureau of Certification Services (BCS)
- **URL:** https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dhs/resources/for-providers/child-care-for-providers
- Regional Child Development Early Learning offices and Early Learning Resource Centers (ELRCs) handle certification and technical assistance.

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **Human Services Code**, Articles IX & X (62 P.S. §§ 901-922, 1001-1087) — enabling statute
- **55 Pa. Code Chapter 3270** — Child Care Centers (7+ children from 2+ families, non-home)
- **55 Pa. Code Chapter 3280** — Group Child Care Homes (7–12 children operating in a residence)
- **55 Pa. Code Chapter 3290** — Family Child Care Homes (4–6 children in operator's residence)
- **55 Pa. Code Chapter 20** — Licensure or Approval procedures (cross-cutting)
- **Act 73 of 2007** — Child Protective Services Law (CPSL) background-check requirements
- **28 Pa. Code Chapter 23** — School / Child Care immunization requirements (cross-reference)

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Child Care Center (CCC)** — non-residential setting, 7+ unrelated children
- **Group Child Care Home (GCCH)** — operator's residence, 7–12 children
- **Family Child Care Home (FCCH)** — operator's residence, 4–6 children
- **Part-day school-age programs** over 3 hrs/day, 90+ days/yr (must be certified as CCC unless explicitly exempted)
- **Head Start / Pre-K Counts / Keystone STARS participants** — additional OCDEL quality standards overlay

## Key Licensing Forms
- **CD 92** — Application for Certificate of Compliance (new or renewal)
- **CY 113** — Child Abuse History Clearance (PA Childline) — required for all staff, volunteers, household members
- **SP4-164** — PA State Police Criminal History Record Check
- **FBI fingerprint-based clearance** (through DHS Cogent)
- **CD 51** — Child Enrollment/Health Record
- **CD 54** — Emergency Contact & Authorization for Medical Care
- **CY 47** — Report of Suspected Child Abuse
- **CD 94** — Staff Application & Orientation Record
- **CD 95** — Staff Medical Assessment & TB screening
- **CY 113** + Act 153 disclosure statement — hiring package
- **Certificate of Occupancy** — issued by local municipality (pre-licensure)
- **Fire Marshal Certificate** — from local fire authority

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (§ 3270.51 Similar Age Level)
| Age band | Ratio | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Infant (birth–1 yr) | 1:4 | 8 |
| Young toddler (1–2 yr) | 1:5 | 10 |
| Older toddler (2–3 yr) | 1:6 | 12 |
| Preschool (3–kindergarten) | 1:10 | 20 |
| Young school-age (K–3rd grade) | 1:12 | 24 |
| Older school-age (4th grade–15 yr) | 1:15 | 30 |

- Mixed-age groups use the ratio for the youngest child (§ 3270.52)
- Nap-time ratios (§ 3270.55) may be doubled for preschool/school-age if other staff are immediately available
- **Minimum 2 staff persons on site any time a child is present** (§ 3270.13)

## Required Child Records (§§ 3270.181–3270.185)
- Child Enrollment form (CD 51) signed by parent
- Emergency contact & pick-up authorization (CD 54)
- **Immunization record** per PA School Code (DTaP, polio, MMR, Hib, hep B, varicella, PCV, influenza)
- Physical exam / health assessment prior to admission, updated annually
- Allergy & chronic-condition care plan
- Developmental/behavioral concerns documentation
- Daily attendance record
- Authorization for emergency medical treatment
- Transportation permission and vehicle information
- Field-trip permission slips
- Medication administration log
- Accident/incident report log

## Required Staff Records (§§ 3270.191–3270.202)
- **PA Child Abuse History Clearance (CY 113)** — renewed every 60 months
- **PA State Police Criminal Record Check** — renewed every 60 months
- **FBI fingerprint-based federal criminal check** — renewed every 60 months
- Staff application and job description
- Staff medical assessment with TB screening within 60 days of hire, updated biennially
- Orientation record (completed within 90 days)
- **6 hours of approved child-care training annually** (§ 3270.31); directors have higher requirements
- **First Aid / CPR** certification — at least one staff person on duty, including infant/child CPR
- **Fire safety training** annually
- **Mandated reporter training** — 3 hours at hire, 2 hours biennial renewal (Act 31 of 2014)
- **Pediatric abusive head trauma training** (infants/toddlers staff)
- Professional Development Registry record — PA Key (Pennsylvania Professional Development Registry)
- Credentials matching role (CDA, ECE associate's/bachelor's, PA School-Age Professional Credential, etc.)

## Inspection Cadence
- **Annual renewal inspection** — announced, conducted by regional DHS BCS office (§ 3270.11(d))
- **Unannounced monitoring inspection** — performed at any time per § 3270.24(b)
- **Complaint-driven inspection** — in response to any allegation
- **Fire safety inspection** — at least annually by local or state fire authority
- **Provisional certificate** monitoring — more frequent during provisional periods
- Inspection summaries are posted in the **Quarterly Licensing Report**

## Renewal Cadence
- **Certificate of Compliance** is issued for **up to 12 months** (§ 3270.11(d)) — i.e., **annual renewal**
- Must apply for renewal before current certificate expires

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Keystone STARS** — PA's QRIS with STAR 1–4 ratings (voluntary but tied to subsidy rates)
- **Pennsylvania Pre-K Counts** — state pre-K at participating providers
- **PA Head Start State Supplemental Assistance Program (HSSAP)**
- **Rising STARS Tuition Assistance** — PD support
- **Act 31/ Act 126** — mandatory mandated-reporter renewal every 5 years
- **Act 153 of 2014** — expanded CPSL background-check universe to all staff, volunteers, and household members 14+
- **Radon testing** — required every 3 years in certain Pennsylvania radon-risk zones; result posted with license
- **Monthly fire safety inspection** — required for all licensed facilities (recent change)
- **Clearances must be on-site and available for inspection**
- All centers must enroll in and report staff through **PA Key/Pennsylvania Professional Development Registry**
- **Early Learning Resource Centers (ELRCs)** — regional hubs (ELRC 1–19) handle STARS, subsidy, and CCDBG interface
