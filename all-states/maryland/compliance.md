# Maryland Child Care Compliance

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** Maryland State Department of Education (MSDE)
- **Division:** Division of Early Childhood (DEC)
- **Branch:** Office of Child Care (OCC), Licensing Branch (13 regional offices)
- **URL:** https://earlychildhood.marylandpublicschools.org/child-care-providers/licensing

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **COMAR 13A.16** — Child Care Centers
- **COMAR 13A.15** — Family Child Care Homes
- **COMAR 13A.17** — Letters of Compliance (public school / recreational programs)
- **COMAR 13A.18** — Large Family Child Care Homes
- **Enabling statute:** Md. Code, Education Article, Title 9.5

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Child Care Center** (COMAR 13A.16) — non-home, ≥ 9 children
- **Family Child Care Home** (COMAR 13A.15) — up to 8 children in a residence, only 2 under 2
- **Large Family Child Care Home** (COMAR 13A.18) — 9–12 children in a residence
- **Letter of Compliance programs** (COMAR 13A.17) — public school recreational / before-after school

## Key Licensing Forms
- **OCC 1201** — Application for a Child Care Center License
- **OCC 1207** — Fire Marshal Inspection
- **OCC 1203** — Background Clearance Application (CBC)
- **OCC 1214** — Health Inventory (child)
- **OCC 1205** — Emergency Form
- **OCC 1216** — Medication Authorization
- **OCC 1215** — Individual Child Care Plan (special health needs)
- **OCC 1228** — Child Care Center Annual License Renewal
- **OCC 1231** — Criminal Background Clearance (fingerprint referral)
- **Maryland EXCELS** QRIS enrollment forms

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (COMAR 13A.16.08.03 — centers)
| Age band | Ratio | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Infant (6 weeks–18 mo) | 1:3 | 6 |
| Toddler (18 mo–2 yr) | 1:3 | 9 |
| 2 years | 1:6 | 12 |
| 3 years | 1:10 | 20 |
| 4 years | 1:10 | 20 |
| 5 years (K) | 1:12 | — |
| School age (≥ 6 yrs) | 1:15 | 30 |

Mixed-age groups containing infants/toddlers: follow youngest-child ratio; with preschoolers and school-agers combined, 1:10 applies and no more than 6 two-year-olds in a group of 13–20.

**Family Child Care:** 1 provider for ≤ 8 children (no more than 2 under 2 and no more than 4 under age 2 with assistant); Large Family = up to 12 with 2 adults.

## Required Child Records
- Application form & emergency contact info
- Immunization record (MDH Form 896 equivalent) per COMAR 10.06.04.03
- Lead testing documentation (Maryland law requires for ages 1 & 2 in targeted ZIPs)
- Physical examination / health inventory (Form OCC 1214) within 60 days of enrollment
- Individual Child Care Plan for children with special needs/allergies
- Medication Authorization (Form OCC 1216)
- Signed policy acknowledgments (parent handbook, discipline, etc.)
- Daily attendance record

## Required Staff Records
- **Criminal Background Check (CBC)** — fingerprint-based state + FBI; renewed every 5 years; includes Child Protective Services clearance
- **Sex Offender Registry** check
- **45-hour Preschool / 45-hour School Age / 90-hour Senior Staff** qualifying courses (MSDE-approved) depending on role
- **Child Development Associate (CDA)** or equivalent early-childhood credential for lead teachers
- **Director qualifications:** 90-hour Child Care Course, experience, and administrative training (per COMAR 13A.16.06)
- **First Aid + CPR** current (pediatric)
- **Continuing training:** minimum **12 clock hours annually** covering core knowledge areas
- **Medication Administration Training (MAT)** if dispensing medication
- **Communicable Disease training** (SIDS / infant safe sleep, shaken baby, emergency preparedness)
- **TB screening** per local requirement
- **Mandated Reporter** status — all child care staff

## Inspection Cadence
- **Annual unannounced inspections** by regional OCC licensing specialists
- **Complaint investigations** as triggered
- **Fire Marshal inspection** annually
- **Environmental Health (local)** as required
- Inspection results posted publicly on CheckCCMD.org

## Renewal Cadence
- **License valid for 3 years** (centers); family homes issued as **registration** also valid for 3 years
- Renewal requires inspection, updated clearances, and continuing ed documentation

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Maryland EXCELS** — 5-tier QRIS; published ratings and linked to subsidy tiered-reimbursement
- **CheckCCMD.org** — mandatory public publication of inspection findings since Jan 2011
- **Breakfast After the Bell / Summer Food Service Program** alignment for subsidy-accepting programs
- **Lead poisoning prevention** — facilities must verify lead screening occurred
- **Emergency Preparedness & Disaster Plan** required with annual drills
- **Safe Sleep Policy** (SIDS reduction) — written, staff-trained
- **Shaken Baby Syndrome** training mandatory
- **Breastfeeding-friendly** accommodations required
- **Child Care Scholarship Program** (subsidy) has additional requirements beyond licensing
- **Continuing Professional Development Registry (CPDR)** — MSDE-maintained professional registry; all staff must be listed
- **13 Regional Licensing Offices** administer the program; each has a designated specialist
