6. Violations & Inspection Data

Primary sources

  • Ohio Child Care Search: childcaresearch.ohio.gov — per-program inspection tab (/inspections/{programId}) with every visit listed and each linked to a PDF.
  • Inspection PDFs: deterministic URL pattern https://childcaresearch.ohio.gov/pdf/{programId}_{YYYY-MM-DD}_{TYPE}.pdf — types observed: MONITOR, COMPLAINT, INSPECTION, RENEWAL, INITIAL.

Data format

Each inspection is a PDF with rule citations (e.g. OAC 5180:2-12-16(A)(3)), Ohio-specific severity tiers: SRNC Serious Risk Non-Compliance (6 points; parent-notice required within 15 business days), MRNC Moderate Risk Non-Compliance (3 points), Low-Risk (1 point). Point sum feeds the program's compliance score on the public portal.

Freshness

  • OCLQS → public portal sync: within 24 hours
  • SRNC parent notification: 15 business days

Scraping / access strategy

  • Option A: Complete the /export email-OTC challenge, receive bulk CSV (all currently-licensed programs + compliance scores).
  • Option B (existing): Paginated HTML scrape — empty-filter search (?county=-1), walk 406 pages, 20 per page.
  • Option C: From the Program IDs, fetch PDF inspections directly using the deterministic URL pattern; OCR for rule citations and risk tiers.
  • No CAPTCHA or auth on inspection PDFs; rate limit 1–2 req/sec.

FOIA path

Ohio Public Records Act (ORC § 149.43). Contact DCY at childrenandyouth.ohio.gov/about-us/contact. 5–10 business-day turnaround. Useful for un-redacted complaint files, pre-2020 historical inspections, SRNC parent notices.

7. Outreach Signals

  • SRNC (Serious Risk Non-Compliance) in last 90 days (requires PDF OCR; highest intent).
  • Compliance score in bottom quartile (available after /export CSV).
  • ≥2 MRNC findings in trailing 12 months (requires PDF OCR).
  • Repeat non-compliance (same rule cited ≥2 times) (requires PDF OCR).
  • SUTQ star rating downgrade (available — star level on public record).
