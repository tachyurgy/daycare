# Georgia — Child Care Compliance Reference

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** Georgia Department of Early Care and Learning (DECAL), commonly branded as **Bright from the Start**
- **Division:** Child Care Services (CCS) — Licensing
- **URL:** https://www.decal.ga.gov/
- Provider portal: https://providers.decal.ga.gov/
- Public search: https://families.decal.ga.gov/ChildCare/Search
- Residential child-care facilities (24-hour care, shelters, group homes) are separately regulated by the **Georgia Department of Human Services (DHS) Residential Child Care Licensing** — NOT DECAL.

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **O.C.G.A. Title 20, Chapter 1A** — Department of Early Care and Learning enabling statute
- **O.C.G.A. § 20-1A-2** — definitions including "Child Care Learning Center" and "Family Child Care Learning Home"
- **Ga. Comp. R. & Regs., Rule Chapter 591-1-1** — Rules for Child Care Learning Centers (CCLCs)
- **Ga. Comp. R. & Regs., Rule Chapter 591-1-2** — Rules for Family Child Care Learning Homes (FCCLHs)
- **Rule Chapter 591-1-3** — Georgia's Pre-K Program
- **Rule Chapter 591-1-4** — Summer Transition Program

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Child Care Learning Center (CCLC)** — 7+ unrelated children in non-residential setting (licensed by DECAL)
- **Family Child Care Learning Home (FCCLH)** — 3–6 unrelated children in operator's residence
- **Exempt Only** — certain short-duration and religious programs (registered but exempt from full licensure)
- **Georgia's Pre-K (Lottery funded)** — participating CCLCs and schools
- **Georgia Head Start** / **Early Head Start** providers
- **Local School System preschools**, **Department of Defense programs**, **University programs**, **Technical College child development programs** — each with separate sub-rules

## Key Licensing Forms
- **Application for Child Care Learning Center License** (DECAL)
- **Application for Family Child Care Learning Home License**
- **Director/Provider Background Check Application** (Comprehensive Records Check – CRC)
- **Fingerprint (FBI / GBI) Background Check** — via IdentoGO
- **Staff Background Check Application**
- **Form M25** — Child Medical/Immunization Certificate (Form 3231)
- **Form 3300** — Hearing, Vision, and Dental screening
- **Child Enrollment Form / Emergency Contact Form**
- **Fire Marshal / Local Fire Safety Inspection Certificate**
- **Health Department / Sanitation Inspection (if serving food)**
- **Certificate of Occupancy** — from local municipality

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (Rule 591-1-1-.32)
| Age band | Ratio | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Infants (< 18 months) | 1:6 | 12 |
| Walking 1-year-olds | 1:8 | 16 |
| 2-year-olds | 1:10 | 20 |
| 3-year-olds | 1:15 | 30 |
| 4-year-olds | 1:18 | 36 |
| 5-year-olds | 1:20 | 40 |
| 6+ years (school-age) | 1:25 | 50 |

- Staff must be seated within arm's length of children under 36 months during meals
- Outdoor playground and ≤2-hour special events are exempt from group-size limits
- Nap-time: ratios may be doubled for 3+ with one staff member in room
- Evening/overnight ratios may be doubled when children are sleeping, but all required staff must be on premises

## Required Child Records (Rule 591-1-1-.20)
- Enrollment form with parent/guardian, pick-up authorization, emergency contacts
- **Form 3231 (Georgia Immunization Certificate)** — per Ga. DPH schedule
- **Form 3300 (Ear, Eye, and Dental Certificate)** — required before or shortly after admission
- Child's physical examination / medical statement
- Allergy, chronic-condition, and medication administration plan
- Behavioral/developmental assessment records
- Daily attendance record
- Field trip and transportation permission slips
- Incident/accident report file
- Medication administration log (written parent authorization required)

## Required Staff Records (Rule 591-1-1-.09)
- **DECAL Comprehensive Records Check (CRC)** — child abuse registry + sex offender + criminal
- **GBI / FBI fingerprint-based background check**
- **Re-check every 5 years**
- Staff application with references and orientation record
- **TB screening** at hire
- **Infant/child/adult CPR & first aid** — all staff with direct child contact (continuous coverage)
- **Orientation training** (40 hours within first 90 days for new center staff in some roles)
- **Minimum 10 clock hours of annual ongoing training** for staff caring for children (Directors have higher — 20 hours)
- **Safe sleep training** (annual)
- **Shaken baby syndrome / abusive head trauma** training
- **Mandated reporter training** (annual)
- **Georgia Professional Development Registry (GaPDS)** — required enrollment for all staff
- **Director credential** meeting Rule 591-1-1-.10 (ECE associate's, CCLC Director Credential, or equivalent)
- Annual **health & safety refresher training** per CCDBG

## Inspection Cadence
- **Minimum one unannounced inspection per year** (more frequent for new licensees or complaint-prone programs)
- **Additional monitoring visits** for Georgia's Pre-K, Head Start, and SUTQ participating programs
- **Complaint investigation inspections** at any time
- **Fire safety inspection** — annual by local or state Fire Marshal
- **Health/sanitation inspection** (for food prep) — annual by county health department
- **Re-licensure inspection** during annual renewal

## Renewal Cadence
- License is issued and must be renewed **annually** (both CCLCs and FCCLHs are valid for 1 year)

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Quality Rated** — Georgia's QRIS, 1–3 Star rating; prerequisite for higher subsidy rates and Georgia's Pre-K participation
- **Georgia's Pre-K Program** — lottery-funded universal pre-K for 4-year-olds; participating centers receive additional requirements and monitoring
- **CAPS (Childcare and Parent Services)** — subsidy program administered by DECAL (payment rates tied to Quality Rated)
- **Summer Transition Program** — summer-camp style program for rising K
- **Background Check Certificate** must be posted
- **Required posting** of license, capacity, Emergency Plan, menu, Immunization Certificate
- **DECAL Scholars** — professional-development scholarship for staff
- Each location must have a **current "Operation Log"** retained 1 year
- **Emergency preparedness / evacuation plan** with quarterly drills
- Centers must maintain **liability insurance** documentation (recommended; required for subsidy/Pre-K participation)
- Posting of **Sanitary Practices** and **Illness Exclusion Policy** in each room
- **Pesticide Application notice** — 24 hours prior to application
- **Transportation rules** — special seating/restraint and attendance checks
