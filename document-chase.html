The Problem

You Know Exactly What This Feels Like

Every director has spent more time chasing documents than actually running their program. It's the worst part of the job.

The Endless Follow-Up

You ask for a document. Nothing happens. You ask again. And again. You leave notes in cubbies, send texts from your personal phone, corner parents at pickup. It takes five touches to get one piece of paper.

~5.2 follow-ups per missing document

The Invisible Gaps

A CPR card expired two weeks ago and nobody noticed. A new hire's background check came back but the affidavit was never signed. You don't find out until someone pulls the file—or worse, the inspector does.

Avg. 3.4 expired docs per facility at any time

The Scale Problem

15 staff members with 8 required documents each. 76 enrolled children with 6 required forms each. That's 576 documents to track, each with its own expiration date. No human can keep that in their head.

576+ documents for a mid-size center

The Chase Engine

Persistent, Professional, Relentless

The system doesn't forget, doesn't get busy, and doesn't feel awkward about the third reminder. It follows up so you don't have to.

Smart Escalation Sequences

Starts with a friendly email. Follows up with a text. Escalates the tone gradually—never rude, always professional, increasingly urgent. Configurable cadence from gentle to firm.

  • Day 1: Friendly initial email with secure upload link
  • Day 3: SMS reminder with direct upload link
  • Day 7: Follow-up email emphasizing deadline
  • Day 14: Final notice with escalation to director

State-Aware Document Intelligence

The system knows exactly what your state requires for each role and each child's age group. It doesn't just track what you tell it—it knows what's missing before you do.

  • FL: Form 680 immunization schedules by child age
  • TX: 24-hour pre-service training breakdown for new hires
  • CA: LIC 700/701/702 series with age-specific requirements
  • Automatic expiration tracking for CPR, background checks, training hours

Secure Upload Portal

Every message includes a unique, time-limited link to a branded upload portal. No accounts to create, no apps to install. Parents and staff tap the link, snap a photo or attach a file, done.

  • Branded with your facility name and logo
  • Works on any phone—camera capture or file upload
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest (AES-256)
  • Automatic document type detection and filing

Expiration Prediction

Document Chase doesn't wait for things to expire. It starts the renewal process weeks before expiration, so the new document is in hand before the old one lapses. Zero-gap compliance.

  • Configurable lead times: 90, 60, 30 day advance notice
  • Expiration forecasting across all staff and child files
  • Priority scoring: most critical expirations surface first
  • Historical patterns predict who needs extra lead time
