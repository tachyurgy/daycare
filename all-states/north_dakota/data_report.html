7. Outreach Signals

  • Biennial license cycle — centers/groups renew every 24 months. Half the base is in a renewal window at any given time; lower absolute trigger frequency but predictable.
  • 24 hrs/year training floor for center staff — above the federal CCDBG floor — creates continuous PD-tracking pressure.
  • Two monitoring visits per year (1 announced + 1 unannounced) — double the buyer touch-points vs. most states.
  • Self-Declaration tier — unique ND category (micro-providers with background-checks but no full license); valuable for Starter-tier segment targeting.
  • Fargo-centric market — F-W-F metro is the single largest cluster; concentrating outreach there yields the highest ROI first quarter.
  • 2023 CCL System rollout — providers are still adapting to the new online portal; inbound goodwill toward "tools that help" is measurably higher in the first 24 months post-system-change.
