6.Violations & Inspection Data

SC publishes per-provider compliance history directly on the DSS SC Child Care Services website. Each provider detail page exposes a Compliance Violations table with date, regulation, severity (High / Medium / Low), and resolution status, covering the previous 36 months.

Full details: violations.md

  • Per-provider URL — scchildcare.org/provider/{id}/{slug}/
  • Children's Trust of SC investigation (2021): 8,537 serious violations over 4 years; top offenders — improper supervision (3,044 high-severity) & out-of-ratio (2,605) — scchildren.org
  • No bulk CSV export; per-facility HTML scrape is reliable.

7.Outreach Signals

Regulatory pain points

  • SLED + FBI + Central Registry checks must clear before staff contact — no conditional starts.
  • 15 hrs annual in-service training per staff member.
  • Health Assessment (DSS 2926) required for operator + all caregivers + household members 16+.
  • Posted license, latest inspection report, evacuation plan, complaint phone must be visible to parents.
  • BRIGHT Act (2019) limits expulsion/suspension — written policy required.

Market heat & channel

  • Children's Trust of SC report surfaced chronic violations; media attention drives director urgency.
  • ABC Quality participation mandatory for SC Voucher (subsidy) providers — quality tier upgrade is a budget line.
  • Columbia (state capital), Charleston, Greenville, Rock Hill, Myrtle Beach = top 5 MSAs to target first.
  • DSS regional offices answer individual questions — channel for ComplianceKit partnerships.
