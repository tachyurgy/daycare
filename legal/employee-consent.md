## For Daycare Staff Uploading Your Own Records

Your employer (the daycare you work at) is using **ComplianceKit** to manage staff compliance records, including certifications, trainings, and other regulatory documents. Before you upload anything about yourself, please take a minute to read this.

### Who is ComplianceKit?

ComplianceKit is the compliance software your employer uses. We are a separate company from your employer. Your employer asked us to store and track these records on their behalf.

### What you are being asked to upload

Your employer will tell you exactly what they need. Typical examples include:

- CPR and First Aid certifications
- State-required early childhood education training certificates
- Background check clearance confirmations (the fact that it was cleared and the date — **not the underlying background check report itself**)
- Professional development and continuing education hours
- Signed handbook acknowledgments
- Food handler permits

Please upload only what your employer asked for. Do not upload documents that contain your Social Security number, bank information, full date of birth with driver's license, or other sensitive information the service is not designed for.

### Who can see what you upload

- **Your employer.** The daycare's administrator(s) can see your uploaded records as part of their regulatory recordkeeping obligations.
- **ComplianceKit.** We store your documents securely and act on your employer's instructions.
- **A small set of trusted vendors.** We use them to host files, send notifications, and perform automated text extraction. See the [Sub-processors](./subprocessors.md) page for the current list.

ComplianceKit does **not** sell your information. ComplianceKit does **not** share your documents with anyone outside your employer and our listed sub-processors, except as required by law.

### Automated Text Extraction (OCR) — You Consent

When you upload a certificate, our system automatically reads the text of the document to pull out useful fields (certificate name, issuing organization, issue date, expiration date). This is called **Optical Character Recognition (OCR)**. We use third-party AI services (currently Mistral AI and Google Gemini, as listed on our Sub-processors page) to perform this extraction.

By uploading, you consent to this automated processing. The AI vendors do not retain your document beyond the processing window and do not use it to train their public AI models, per our contracts with them.

### How long your records are kept

ComplianceKit retains your uploaded records for as long as you are employed at the daycare, **plus up to 7 years** after your employment ends, to help your employer meet tax, workers' compensation, and regulatory recordkeeping requirements. After that period, records are deleted unless legally required to be kept longer.

Your employer — not ComplianceKit — ultimately determines retention periods based on applicable law. If you have questions about retention, ask your employer first.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **See** what ComplianceKit stores about you.
- **Correct** anything that is inaccurate.
- **Request deletion** of information (subject to your employer's legal recordkeeping obligations).
- **Get a copy** in a common electronic format.

The fastest way to exercise these rights is to ask your employer. If you need ComplianceKit's help, email {{LEGAL_EMAIL}}.

### Security

We protect your records with strong encryption, strict access controls, and documented security practices. If a security incident affects your data, we will notify your employer within 72 hours, and your employer will notify you as required by state law.

### Questions

- Ask your employer first — they can help you directly.
