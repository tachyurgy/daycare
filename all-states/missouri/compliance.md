# Missouri Child Care Compliance

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** Missouri Department of Elementary and Secondary Education (DESE)
- **Division:** Office of Childhood (consolidated 2021)
- **Section:** Section for Child Care Regulation (SCCR)
- **URL:** https://dese.mo.gov/childhood/child-care

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **5 CSR 25-500** (current, effective 2022) — Licensing Rules for Child Care Centers, Group Child Care Homes, and Family Child Care Homes (migrated from 19 CSR 30-62 when licensing moved from DHSS to DESE)
- **5 CSR 25-400** — Definitions & General Provisions
- **5 CSR 25-600** — Child Care Subsidy
- **Enabling statute:** RSMo Chapter 210 (§ 210.201–.257)

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Licensed Child Care Center** — facility with 11+ children (generally non-residential)
- **Licensed Group Child Care Home** — 11–20 children in an operator's residence
- **Licensed Family Child Care Home** — up to 10 children in operator's residence (varies by ages)
- **License-exempt Child Care** — religiously affiliated, nursery schools, certain relatives, short-duration programs (must still register for subsidy participation)

## Key Licensing Forms
- **CCFS-1 — Application for a Child Care License**
- **CCFS-100 — Staff Member Record**
- **CCFS-101 — Individual Medical Examination Report (Staff)**
- **CCFS-200 — Child's Health & Emergency Form**
- **CCFS-201 — Immunization Record**
- **CCFS-202 — Medication Authorization**
- **CCFS-300 — Incident / Injury Report**
- **Family Care Safety Registry (FCSR)** background check registration
- **Fire safety inspection (State Fire Marshal)** and **environmental/health inspection**

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (5 CSR 25-500.112 — centers)
| Age band | Ratio | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Birth–2 years (mixed infant/toddler) | 1:4 | 8 |
| 2 years (solely) | 1:8 | 16 |
| 3 years | 1:10 | — |
| 4 years | 1:10 | — |
| 3–4 years mixed | 1:10 | — |
| 5+ years (kindergarten / school age) | 1:16 | — |

Mixed-age groups containing infants: ratio defaults to infant ratio (1:4).

**Family Child Care Home:** up to 10 children with no more than 4 under age 2 (including provider's own under 11).

## Required Child Records
- Enrollment application with parent/emergency contacts and authorized pick-up list
- **Current immunization record** — per Missouri DHSS schedule (Form CCFS-201)
- **Physical examination** or signed parent statement of health within 30 days of enrollment
- **Medical / allergy action plans**
- Signed authorizations (transport, photo, emergency, medication)
- Daily attendance log
- **Safe Sleep signed policy** for infant rooms

## Required Staff Records
- **Family Care Safety Registry (FCSR)** background screening — initial & ongoing monitoring (every 2 years)
- **Fingerprint-based FBI criminal background check** — required under federal CCDBG
- **Missouri Child Abuse/Neglect Central Registry** clearance
- **Sex Offender Registry** check
- **Medical examination** (within 12 months of employment) — Form CCFS-101
- **TB screening** (current)
- **CPR + First Aid** certification (pediatric)
- **12 clock hours annual continuing education** (minimum; more for directors)
- **Pre-service orientation** before being counted in ratio
- **CCDBG 10-topic health & safety training** (pre-service)
- **Safe Sleep, SIDS, Shaken Baby Syndrome** training
- Director qualifications: per 5 CSR 25-500.182 (varies by capacity — 30 college credits with 6 child-related, OR CDA, OR experience)

## Inspection Cadence
- **At least one unannounced annual inspection** for licensed programs (federal CCDBG mandate)
- **Initial licensing inspection** on application
- **Complaint investigations** as triggered
- **Fire safety inspection** annually (State Fire Marshal)
- Inspection history available via Show Me Child Care Provider Search

## Renewal Cadence
- **License valid for 2 years** for centers and group homes; **2 years** for family homes
- Renewal requires compliance inspection, updated clearances, insurance verification

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Missouri Quality Rating System (Show Me Quality Care)** — optional voluntary QRIS; lesser developed than peer-state systems
- **Show Me Child Care Provider Search** (healthapps.dhss.mo.gov/childcaresearch/) — public-facing directory with inspection/compliance history
- **T.E.A.C.H. Early Childhood® MISSOURI** scholarship program — incentives for CDA/AA/BA attainment
- **Family Care Safety Registry (FCSR)** — unique Missouri pre-employment screening clearinghouse covering child care + elder care + in-home workers
- **2021 transition:** licensing authority moved from DHSS to DESE Office of Childhood; citations updated from 19 CSR 30-62 to 5 CSR 25-500
- **Religious exemption** — religious schools can be exempt from licensing but must register with DESE
- **Subsidy-contracted providers** must meet additional requirements beyond basic licensing
- **Emergency preparedness** — plan with quarterly drills (fire, tornado, intruder)
- **Tornado drills** (Missouri-specific) — required periodically during tornado season
- **Safe Sleep / ABC** rules mandatory for infant programs
