6. Violations & Inspection Data

Primary sources

  • COMPASS Provider Search → "View Inspection Summary": compass.dhs.pa.gov/providersearch — per-facility inspection history with regulation citations, violation narratives, and Plan of Correction status.
  • DHS Quarterly Licensing Report: pa.gov quarterly report — PDF summaries of provisional licenses, revocations, and illegal operators.
  • Find Child Care PA (alternate URL): findchildcare.pa.gov — redirects to COMPASS search.

Data format

COMPASS is an ASP.NET MVC app; no public API. Inspection detail is available via HTML (sometimes PDF) behind the View Inspection Summary link. Quarterly Licensing Report is published as a plain PDF per quarter.

Legal basis for public disclosure: Act 24 of 2011 (amendment to the Public Welfare Code).

Freshness

  • COMPASS: inspection reports typically posted 30–60 days after visit
  • Quarterly Licensing Report: 2–3 months after quarter close

Scraping / access strategy

  • Seed with ajn5-kaxt (7,473 facility IDs) already in pennsylvania_leads.csv.
  • Resolve each ID to https://www.compass.dhs.pa.gov/providersearch/Home/ProviderDetails/{FacilityId} with a session cookie; fetch InspectionSummary/{FacilityId} for the violation table.
  • Rate limit to ≈1 req/sec; ~4 hours single-threaded for full state.
  • Quarterly PDF: fetch latest from Quarterly Licensing Report landing; OCR the OCDEL section for Provisional/Revoked tables — these are the hot leads.

FOIA path

PA Right-to-Know Law (65 P.S. §§ 67.101–67.3104). Request to DHS at RA-PWOpenRecords@pa.gov — 5-business-day response window. DHS has granted CSV-formatted CII exports in past RTKL fulfillments.

7. Outreach Signals

  • Provisional Certificate of Compliance issued (available — quarterly PDF OCR; strongest intent signal).
  • License Revocation pending / recently posted (available — quarterly PDF).
  • Recent Complaint Investigation with cited violations (available — COMPASS scrape).
  • Annual renewal inspection due within 90 days (available — ajn5-kaxt has Certification End Date).
  • Keystone STARS downgrade (available — STARS level on public record; compare month-over-month).
