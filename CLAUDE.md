# ComplianceKit - Project Context

## What is ComplianceKit?

Compliance management SaaS for child care providers (daycare centers, family child care homes). It replaces paper binders, spreadsheets, and panic-before-inspection workflows with a single platform that tracks every state-specific regulatory requirement, deadline, and document.

**One-liner:** "Be inspection-ready every single day."

## Target Market

- **ICP:** Single-site child care centers in **Florida, Texas, and California** (~80,000 licensed facilities combined)
- **Buyer persona:** Owner/director who feels compliance pain directly, makes purchasing decisions fast
- **Price point:** $99/mo Professional plan (primary), $49/mo Starter, $199/site/mo Enterprise
- **ARPA:** ~$99/mo | **LTV:** ~$17,000 (7% annual churn)

## Core Product Features

- **Compliance Dashboard** - real-time compliance score (0-100), critical alerts, 90-day deadline timeline
- **Child File Management** - state-specific document checklists, immunization tracking, parent upload portal
- **Staff File Management** - certification expiration engine, training hour tracker, background check status
- **Facility & Operations** - digital daily safety checklists, drill scheduler/logger, wall posting tracker, ratio calculator
- **Inspection Readiness** - self-inspection simulator (mirrors actual state inspector checklists), violation risk assessment, inspection-ready reports
- **Notifications** - multi-channel alerts (email, SMS, push) at 90/60/30-day thresholds

## Go-to-Market Strategy

- **Content-led SEO growth** targeting high-intent queries ("how to pass daycare inspection [state]", "how to start a daycare [state]", immunization requirements)
- **Zero CAC** - articles demonstrate expertise, CTAs convert to product
- **State-specific content already built** for CA, TX, FL (see HTML files in this directory)

## Key State Regulatory Knowledge

Each state has deep research completed (see `planning-docs/state-docs/`):
- **California:** Community Care Licensing (Title 22), forms LIC-281A, LIC-311A, LIC-421, etc.
- **Texas:** HHSC Minimum Standards (Chapter 746), forms 1100, 2935, 2941, 7259-7263, etc.
- **Florida:** DCF Child Care Facility Handbook (CFOP 170-20), forms CF-FSP 5274/5316, etc.
- Also: Oregon, Washington (expansion states)
