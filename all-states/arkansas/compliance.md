# Arkansas — Child Care Licensing Compliance Reference

_Compiled 2026-04-18 for ComplianceKit. All citations are to publicly-available primary sources — verify before relying in production._

## Licensing Authority

- **Arkansas Department of Human Services (DHS) — Division of Child Care and Early Childhood Education (DCCECE), Child Care Licensing Unit**
  - Note: Arkansas transferred certain Office of Early Childhood functions to the **Division of Elementary & Secondary Education (DESE)** circa 2019 (ABC Pre-K, Better Beginnings); **licensing remains at DHS-DCCECE**.

- Portal: https://humanservices.arkansas.gov (DCCECE)

## Primary Regulatory Framework

- **Arkansas Child Care Facility Licensing Act** — Ark. Code Ann. §§ 20-78-201 through 20-78-223
- **Arkansas Administrative Code Agency 016, Division 22, Rule 016.22.20-005** — Minimum Licensing Requirements (Child Care Centers, Licensed Family Homes, Out-of-School Time, Registered Family Homes)
- **Better Beginnings** QRIS rules at DESE
- **Ark. Code § 20-38-101 et seq.** — Child Care Facility immunizations / health

## Facility Types Regulated

| Type | Scope |
|------|-------|
| Child Care Center | Non-residential, 6+ children |
| Licensed Child Care Family Home | 10 or fewer children in provider's home (licensed, not just registered) |
| Registered Child Care Family Home | Smaller capacity, registration-level oversight |
| Out-of-School Time Facility | Before/after-school programs |
| Night-time Child Care | Overnight care regs apply |

## Key Licensing Forms

- **DCC-100** — Application for License (Center)
- **DCC-200** — Licensed Family Child Care Home Application
- **Registered Family Home Registration** form
- **Background Check Consent** — Arkansas State Police + Arkansas Child Maltreatment Central Registry + FBI fingerprint + sex-offender (under Ark. Code § 20-38)
- **Medical Statement for Staff**
- **Child's Emergency Card**
- **Immunization Record** per Arkansas Department of Health schedule
- **Director Qualifications Form**
- **Fire Marshal Inspection Report**
- **Food Service / Health Inspection Report** (if preparing food)

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes

(Child Care Centers — Minimum Licensing Requirements, 016.22.20-005):

| Age Band | Ratio | Max Group |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| Under 18 months | 1:5 | —* |
| 18 months – 36 months | 1:8 | —* |
| 36 months – 3 years | 1:12 | —* |
| 4 years | 1:15 | —* |
| 5 years | 1:18 | —* |
| School-age (6+) | 1:18 | —* |

\* Arkansas sets ratios but does not set a hard maximum group size in the Minimum Licensing Requirements for most age bands — relies on square-footage + ratio.

**Naptime (children ≥2½ yr):** 50% of required staff may remain with children; additional staff must be available for emergencies.

**Mixed groups:** ratio of the youngest age band applies during lunch / outdoor play if combined.

**Family Child Care Homes:** up to 10 children (≤5 under 3 yrs); 2-adult rule at 7+ children.

## Required Child Records

- Enrollment / emergency form
- **Arkansas Department of Health immunization** record (or Form ID-33 exemption — medical, religious, or philosophical)
- Physical exam within 60 days of enrollment
- Allergy, special-diet, and medication authorization
- Pickup authorization
- Daily attendance log
- Incident/injury log
- Parent handbook acknowledgment

## Required Staff Records

- Application, references, reportable-offense affidavit
- **Arkansas State Police criminal background check + Arkansas Child Maltreatment Central Registry + FBI fingerprint + Adult Maltreatment Registry + Sex Offender Registry** — initial + every 5 years
- Health statement (TB risk assessment)
- Orientation within 30 days
- **Annual in-service training — 15 clock hours minimum** (directors 30 hrs)
  - Includes Better Beginnings required topics, CPR, First Aid, SIDS, mandated reporter
- **CPR + Pediatric First Aid** required for all caregivers working with infants/toddlers; current for at least 1 staff on premises at all times
- Mandated reporter training (Ark. Code § 12-18-402)
- Shaken Baby / Safe Sleep training (infant staff)
- Medication administration training
- Food handler certification (if preparing)

## Inspection Cadence

- **Unannounced inspections 1–3× per year**, depending on program type and compliance history
- Initial + renewal licensing inspections
- Complaint-driven follow-ups
- Fire Marshal + Health Department inspections per statute

## Renewal Cadence

- **Annual renewal.** License must be kept current; fees vary by capacity.
- Renewal requires: passing inspection, current background checks, training compliance, CPR/First Aid certifications, fee payment

## Notable State-Specific Requirements

- **Better Beginnings** QRIS (administered by DESE Office of Early Childhood). Levels 1–3 used to calculate subsidy reimbursement and preferred status with working families.
- **ABC Pre-K / Arkansas Better Chance** — public pre-K program; participating facilities subject to additional DESE standards.
- **Reportable Offenses** under Ark. Code § 20-78-607: permanent disqualification for specified felonies (violent, sexual, drug trafficking).
- **Registered vs. Licensed Family Home** — Arkansas distinguishes the two; registration has lighter requirements but limited capacity.
- **Tornado drills** — monthly during tornado season (March–October), with annual safe-shelter plan.
- **Fire drills** — monthly.
- **Swimming pool / water safety** — strict rules, lifeguard supervision, parent consent.
- **Weapons restriction** — firearms must be locked, ammunition separate, inaccessible to children.
- **Night-time care** — additional staffing and sleep requirements.
- **CACFP alignment** for meal planning if participating in federal food program.
- **Arkansas Traveler Form / ARKIDS First** — used for subsidy enrollment verification; not licensing but often tracked together.
- **Better Beginnings accreditation** can substitute for some renewal inspection items.
