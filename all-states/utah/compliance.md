# Utah — Child Care Licensing Compliance Reference

_Compiled 2026-04-18 for ComplianceKit. All citations are to publicly-available primary sources — verify before relying in production._

## Licensing Authority

- **Utah Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS) — Division of Licensing and Background Checks (DLBC), Office of Child Care Licensing**
- Statewide portal: https://dlbc.utah.gov
- Find-a-Facility tool: https://dlbc.utah.gov/information-for-the-public/find-a-facility/
- Provider portal: https://provider.dlbc.utah.gov
- Formerly DOH Child Care Licensing Program; consolidated under DHHS 2022 reorganization.

## Primary Regulatory Framework

- **Utah Code Title 26B, Chapter 2, Part 4** — Child Care Licensing Act (formerly Utah Code §§ 26-39)
- **Utah Administrative Code Title R381** — Child Care Center Licensing Committee
  - **R381-100** — Child Care Centers
  - **R381-60** — Hourly Child Care Centers
  - **R381-70** — Residential Child Care (Family Child Care Homes)
  - **R381-40** — General provisions & capacity
- **Utah Admin Code R430-8** and R430-90 for related facility/fire

## Facility Types Regulated

| Type | Rule | Scope |
|------|------|-------|
| Licensed Child Care Center | R381-100 | 16+ children in non-residential setting |
| Licensed Hourly Child Care Center | R381-60 | Drop-in / hourly |
| Licensed Family Child Care (residence, 8–16 children) | R381-70 | In provider's home |
| Residential Certificate Family Child Care | R381-70 | 1–8 children, registered |
| License-Exempt Programs | — | Certified only for CCDF subsidy |

## Key Licensing Forms

- **CC License Application** (new / change of ownership / renewal)
- **Facility Self-Assessment Checklist**
- **Director Qualification Form** (minimum age 21, required experience + CDA / ECE credits)
- **Caregiver Qualifications Form** (minimum age 18; 20 hrs pre-service + 10 hrs/yr training)
- **Background Screening Consent** (Utah Office of Licensing Background Processing — fingerprint + Utah Bureau of Criminal Identification + FBI + child/adult abuse registries + sex offender)
- **Medical / TB Statement**
- **Emergency Preparedness Plan** (R381-100-15)
- **Transportation / Vehicle Safety Plan** (if transporting)
- **Inspection Checklists** (posted publicly by DLBC)

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (R381-100-10)

Utah uses a unique "oldest-child-in-group-minus-1-age-band" formula; standard ratios / max group sizes:

| Age Band | Ratio | Max Group |
|----------|-------|-----------|
| Infant (0–12 mo) | 1:4 | 8 |
| Toddler (12–24 mo) | 1:4 | 8 |
| Age 2 | 1:7 | 14 |
| Age 3 | 1:12 | 24 |
| Age 4 | 1:15 | 30 |
| Age 5 (pre-K) | 1:20 | 40 |
| School-age (6–12) | 1:20 | 40 |

**Mixed-age groups:** ratio determined by youngest child; max group determined by age of oldest minus one age band (R381-100-10).

**Nap-time doubling:** permitted for children ≥18 months if restful and a second caregiver is on-site within immediate reach.

**Two-caregiver minimum** when a single group exceeds 8 children or has >2 infants/toddlers.

## Required Child Records

- Admission / enrollment form with emergency contacts
- Utah **immunization record** per Utah Code 53G-9-3 schedule (or exemption form)
- Physical exam / health history
- Allergy & medication plans
- Pickup authorization list
- Daily attendance log
- Incident / injury reports
- Child's individual plan if IEP / IFSP

## Required Staff Records

- Employment application / resume
- **Office of Background Processing clearance** — initial + annual renewal
- TB test or risk assessment
- Orientation (≥8 hrs) within first 5 days of unsupervised contact
- **Pre-service training — 20 hrs** before unsupervised care, including Utah basics, SIDS, shaken baby, infection control
- **Annual training — 10 hrs minimum** (20 for directors) via Care About Childcare registry
- CPR + Pediatric First Aid (required for at least 1 staff at all times)
- Medication administration training
- Mandated reporter acknowledgment (Utah Code 62A-4a-403)

## Inspection Cadence

- **Annual announced inspection** at each facility for license renewal
- **Additional unannounced inspections** for complaints or follow-up
- Compliance history available 36 months back through DLBC Find-a-Facility tool
- Inspection checklist published (2025 revision): https://dlbc.utah.gov/wp-content/uploads/CENTER-Inspection-Checklist-7_2_2025.pdf

## Renewal Cadence

- **Annual license renewal.** DLBC conducts annual announced inspection + fee.
- Renewal tied to passing inspection, current background checks, and training compliance on Care About Childcare registry.

## Notable State-Specific Requirements

- **Care About Childcare (CAC) Professional Development Registry** — all staff must maintain a CAC profile; licensed facilities must use CAC-approved trainings for compliance hours.
- **Background screening** through Utah Office of Background Processing (separate office within DHHS licensing) — consent form, fingerprinting, and clearance letter required BEFORE unsupervised contact.
- **Shaken Baby / AHT training** required within first 30 days for caregivers working with children under 2.
- **Sunscreen & topical** requires written parent consent; diaper creams categorized separately.
- **Utah-specific weapons rule** — firearms must be unloaded, locked, separate ammunition; same applies to caregiver's personal vehicles during transport.
- **Outdoor play area** — fenced, min 40 sq ft per child, shade requirement.
- **Water safety** — specific rules for pools, splash pads, and trips to open water.
- **Wildfire / air-quality** — rules require Air Quality Index monitoring during high-pollution days.
- **QRIS: Care About Childcare Quality Improvement** (Utah's voluntary QRIS tied to subsidy add-ons).
