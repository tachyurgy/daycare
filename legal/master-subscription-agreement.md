## 5. Term and Renewal

### 5.1 Initial Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date of the Order and continues for the period specified in the Order (default: one month).

### 5.2 Auto-Renewal

The Subscription Term automatically renews for successive periods equal to the initial term unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days before the end of the then-current term. For annual subscriptions, we will email a renewal reminder at least 30 days before renewal.

### 5.3 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on 30 days' prior written notice. Customer remains obligated to pay Fees through the effective date of termination; no refund is due for termination for convenience outside the 30-day first-charge window.

### 5.4 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement upon 30 days' written notice of the other party's material breach if the breach is not cured within the notice period. We may terminate immediately and without cure for breaches that pose security or legal risk.

### 5.5 Effect of Termination

Upon termination: (a) Customer's right to access the Service ceases; (b) ComplianceKit will make Customer Data available for export for 30 days; (c) thereafter, ComplianceKit will delete Customer Data in accordance with Section 13 and the DPA; (d) accrued payment obligations survive.

## 7. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

### 7.1 Uptime Target

ComplianceKit targets **99.5% monthly uptime** for the production Service (measured as availability of the customer-facing application excluding Excluded Downtime).

### 7.2 Service Credits

If actual monthly uptime falls below target, Customer's sole and exclusive remedy is a service credit against the next month's Fees as follows:

| Monthly Uptime | Service Credit |
|----------------|----------------|
| ≥ 99.5% | 0% |
| < 99.5% and ≥ 99.0% | 5% of that month's Fees |
| < 99.0% and ≥ 95.0% | 10% of that month's Fees |
| < 95.0% | 25% of that month's Fees |

### 7.3 Excluded Downtime

Uptime excludes: (a) scheduled maintenance announced at least 48 hours in advance; (b) emergency maintenance of reasonable duration; (c) force majeure events; (d) sub-processor outages beyond our reasonable control; (e) issues caused by Customer systems or Customer's misuse.

### 7.4 Credit Request

Customer must request a service credit in writing within 30 days after the end of the affected month. Credits are capped at the Fees for the affected month and expire if the account terminates without applying them.

## 13. Data Return and Deletion

On termination or expiration, ComplianceKit will:

1. Make Customer Data available to Customer for export via the Service (or via a mutually agreed secure transfer mechanism) for 30 days after termination.
2. After the 30-day export window, delete Customer Data from the production environment within a further 60 days, subject to:
   - Retention required by law (e.g., tax records for 7 years).
   - Retention of executed e-signature audit trails for 7 years, as described in the [Signature Audit Trail Schema](./signature-audit-trail-schema.md).
   - Retention of routine backups according to our backup rotation; backups are purged within 90 days of their creation.

Deletion will be carried out in a manner designed to prevent recovery. On request, we will provide written confirmation of deletion.
