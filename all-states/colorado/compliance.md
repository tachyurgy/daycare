# Colorado Child Care Compliance

## Licensing Authority
- **Agency:** Colorado Department of Early Childhood (CDEC) — created 2022 (transferred from CDHS)
- **Division:** Division of Early Learning Licensing and Administration (DELLA)
- **URL:** https://cdec.colorado.gov/for-providers/child-care-licensing-and-administration

## Primary Regulatory Framework
- **12 CCR 2509-8 § 7.702** — Rules Regulating Child Care Centers (less than 24-hour care)
- **12 CCR 2509-8 § 7.707** — Family Child Care Homes (large)
- **12 CCR 2509-8 § 7.708** — Family Child Care Homes (regular)
- **12 CCR 2509-8 § 7.712** — School-Age Child Care Centers
- **12 CCR 2509-8 § 7.701** — General Rules for Child Care Facilities (background checks, reporting, enforcement)
- **Enabling statute:** C.R.S. § 26.5-5-301 et seq.

## Facility Types Regulated
- **Child Care Center** (7.702) — non-residential center serving multiple children
- **Preschool** (subset of Centers)
- **Infant Nursery** (subset of Centers)
- **Large Family Child Care Home** (7.707) — up to 12 children in a residence
- **Family Child Care Home** (7.708) — up to 6 children in a residence (with own children included)
- **Experiential/Neighborhood Youth Organization (NYO)**
- **School-Age Child Care Center** (7.712)
- **Resident Camp** (7.711)
- **Day Treatment, Psychiatric Residential** (separately regulated)

## Key Licensing Forms
- **Application for License to Operate a Child Care Facility** (Form DCEE1001)
- **Medical Statement (Adult & Child)** forms
- **Central Registry of Child Protection (Trails)** background authorization
- **Fingerprint-based FBI / CBI Background Check** submission via CDEC approved vendor
- **Incident Report**
- **Emergency Preparedness Plan**
- **Colorado Shines** QRIS enrollment
- **Immunization Tracking System (CIIS)** access setup
- **CCCAP Fiscal Agreement** for subsidy participation

## Child-to-Staff Ratios & Group Sizes (12 CCR 2509-8 § 7.702.52 — centers)
| Age band | Ratio | Max group size |
|---|---|---|
| Infants 6 weeks–12 mo | 1:5 | 10 |
| Infants 12–18 mo | 1:5 | 10 |
| 18–24 months | 1:5 | 10 |
| 2–3 years | 1:7 | 14 |
| 2.5–3 years | 1:8 | 16 |
| 3 years | 1:10 | 20 |
| 4–5 years | 1:12 | 24 |
| K–first grade (in center) | 1:15 | 30 |
| School age (≥ 6 yr) | 1:15 | 30 |

Mixed-age groups default to ratio of youngest child. Infants may not be combined with children over 30 months in the same group.

**Family Child Care Home:** up to 6 children, including provider's own under 8; no more than 2 under 2.

## Required Child Records
- Enrollment application with emergency contacts
- **Immunization record** — certified in Colorado Immunization Information System (CIIS) or signed exemption
- **Health statement** from licensed healthcare provider within 30 days of enrollment
- **Individual Health Care Plan** for children with special medical needs/allergies
- **Permission forms** for field trips, transportation, photo release
- **Medication consent** (Standing Orders)
- **Daily attendance record** (in/out times with signatures)

## Required Staff Records
- **Fingerprint-based FBI + Colorado Bureau of Investigation background check** — every 5 years
- **Trails / Child Welfare Central Registry check** — Colorado Automated Child Welfare System
- **Sex Offender Registry / National Sex Offender check**
- **Medical statement** (adult) within 30 days of employment
- **TB risk assessment**
- **Colorado Shines Professional Development Information System (PDIS)** account — all staff register
- **Pre-service training** through PDIS (Introduction to Family Child Care / Center Basics; Colorado-approved modules)
- **First Aid + CPR** — current (adult/child/infant) certified in-person (blended acceptable)
- **Standard Precautions, Universal Safety, Emergency Preparedness** training
- **Child Abuse Prevention / Mandated Reporting** training — initial within 30 days + annual refresher
- **Medication Administration** training if dispensing medication
- **Director:** Director Qualification approval (degree + experience matrix in 7.702.45)
- **15 clock-hours annual ongoing training** per staff (logged in PDIS)

## Inspection Cadence
- **Initial inspection** during licensing
- **Routine licensing monitoring visit:** at least once per licensing cycle; high-quality programs may see less frequent visits
- **Unannounced inspection at least annually** per federal CCDBG requirement
- **Complaint-triggered investigations**
- **Fire & building inspections** per local codes
- Public inspection reports at Colorado Child Care Facility Search

## Renewal Cadence
- **Initial license** issued for 1 year (provisional); **standard license** is then **continuous** as long as annual fee paid and compliance maintained
- Colorado moved away from periodic renewal — providers must maintain continuous compliance

## Notable State-Specific Requirements
- **Colorado Shines** — 5-level QRIS; rating published on Colorado Shines portal, tied to CCCAP (subsidy) tiered reimbursement
- **Colorado Shines Professional Development Information System (PDIS)** — mandatory professional registry tracking all early-childhood workforce training
- **Colorado Child Care Assistance Program (CCCAP)** — licensed providers must have Fiscal Agreement to accept subsidy
- **Colorado Preschool Program (CPP) / Universal Preschool** — universal pre-K program (effective 2023) with separate contracting through CDEC
- **Minimum Wage** compliance tracking (CDEC reviews staff pay policies)
- **Healthy Child Care Colorado** — physical activity and nutrition standards
- **Safe Sleep** (SIDS) written policy and annual staff training
- **Shaken Baby Syndrome / Abusive Head Trauma** training required
- **Gun safety / weapons policy** — firearms stored unloaded, locked, separate from ammo
- **Altitude and Mountain Safety** considerations (some CDEC guidance unique to Rocky Mountain programs)
- **CDEC is a 2022-consolidated agency** — all early learning functions moved from CDHS; regulation citations still show 12 CCR 2509-8 (legacy path) but now administered by CDEC DELLA
- **Emergency preparedness plan** with fire + lockdown + evacuation + reunification procedures
- **Gang-affiliated tattoos** disclosure under staff background review (Colorado-specific addition)
