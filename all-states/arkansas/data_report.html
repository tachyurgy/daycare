6. Violations & Inspection Data

Primary source

DHS DCCECE eLicensing on Salesforce Experience Cloud. Single per-provider record exposes: license status, compliance report history (violations + complaints + corrective actions), Better Beginnings QRIS rating.

  • Search: find-providers
  • Complaint submission on same site: elicensing/s/complaint

Format

Salesforce Aura / Lightning 403 to bots No Socrata No ArcGIS — Playwright with real-browser profile required; Aura XHR capture possible but fragile.

Freshness

1–3 unannounced inspections / year + fire + health department layer. Annual renewal drives record churn. Better Beginnings QRIS under separate DESE cadence.

Historical context

Arkansas Online 2018 coverage of a central-Arkansas daycare investigation confirms DHS does release structured inspection findings to journalists (≥4 ratio complaints, monthly monitoring imposed). Arkansas Advocate reported the 2023–2024 DESE database launch — focuses on quality-side data.

FOIA path

Arkansas FOIA (§ 25-19-101 et seq.) — 3-business-day statutory response. DHS runs a NextRequest portal that shows previously-fulfilled requests (useful for scoping). DHS FOIA info. FOIA handbook: 18th edition (2017). Template: NFOIC Arkansas sample.

7. Outreach Signals

  • Better Beginnings level < 3 — subsidy reimbursement differential = clear ROI pitch
  • Substantiated complaint in eLicensing — high-intent buyer
  • Registered vs Licensed Family Home distinction — registered homes upgrading to licensed are high-intent (ComplianceKit accelerates compliance readiness)
  • Reportable Offenses (§ 20-78-607) permanent DQ — staff screening is heavier than most states
  • Tornado drills monthly March–Oct — climate cadence unique to AR / OK / KS / TX
  • ABC Pre-K participating sites — dual-agency oversight (DCCECE + DESE) → tracking burden
  • Salesforce portal friction — providers themselves complain about it; ComplianceKit's "single-pane" message lands well

8. Next-step Data Acquisition

  1. Arkansas FOIA (primary recommended path): 3-business-day response window; request full list of licensed + registered providers + last 24 months of compliance reports + complaint dispositions. Template at NFOIC. File via NextRequest portal.
  2. NextRequest archive mining: review previously-fulfilled DHS requests — inspection datasets may already be publicly posted there.
  3. Playwright + Salesforce Aura intercept (secondary): headless-with-JS; capture Aura XHR payloads; replay with rotating sessions. Expect brittleness — Salesforce rotates endpoints.
  4. DESE Better Beginnings overlay: pull QRIS ratings to cross-reference provider quality data.
  5. Fire Marshal + Health Dept. overlay: inspection data sits at those agencies — FOIA both for a full compliance picture.
