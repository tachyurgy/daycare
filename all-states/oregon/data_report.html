6.Violations & Inspection Data

Oregon has the strongest public-transparency regime in this batch. Find Child Care Oregon and the legacy Child Care Safety Portal expose, per provider: valid findings over the last 5 years, unable-to-substantiate complaints over the last 2 years, serious-injury / death / substantiated-abuse counts, and enforcement activity. Aggregate counts are refreshed quarterly; the annual state-level report is due March 15 each year under CCDBG federal rule.

7.Outreach Signals

Regulatory pain points

  • Central Background Registry (CBR) — staff can't start unsupervised contact without clearance; re-enrollment every 2 years.
  • 15 annual clock hours of training (8 in ECE, 1 in health/safety).
  • Safe Sleep for Oregon Infants within 30 days of hire for infant-room staff.
  • Food Handler Card within 30 days for kitchen / bottle-prep staff.
  • Lead testing of drinking water required; radon testing where applicable.
  • Find Child Care Oregon makes violations visible to parents — directors face reputational pressure.

Market heat & channel

  • Spark QRIS (3-5 star) participants are the highest-quality subset — tied to CCDBG payments.
  • Portland metro dominates volume (581 listed centers on aggregator) — Beaverton, Hillsboro, Gresham key suburbs.
  • Multi-Site Program structure (OAR 414-305-0100) creates natural upsell paths for multi-location owners.
  • DELC was established in 2023 — reorg wave creates friction for providers juggling old ELD forms and new DELC/CCLD forms. ComplianceKit can hide the drift.

8.Next-Step Data Acquisition

  1. File an Oregon Public Records Law request with DELC CCLD (ccld.customerservice@delc.oregon.gov) for the full licensee roster + 24-month valid-finding history in CSV/Excel.
  2. Harvest provider list from WorkLife Systems compliance search (stage.worklifesystems.com) via guest-login session; the URL accepts GET searches once a session cookie is set.
  3. For each provider, scrape compliance detail, download CCLD-0093 Contact Report PDFs, and structure 5-year finding history.
  4. Expand leads coverage beyond Portland metro: Gresham, Bend, Medford, Tigard, Lake Oswego — quick aggregator top-up.
  5. Cross-reference Spark QRIS ratings and use "valid finding in last 24 months + Spark rating ≤ 3" as primary lead-score signal.
