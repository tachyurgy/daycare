Please read this Electronic Signature Consumer Disclosure (this "**Disclosure**") carefully. It explains your rights when signing documents electronically through the ComplianceKit service (the "**Service**") operated by {{COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME}} ("**ComplianceKit**," "**we**," "**us**"). This Disclosure is required by the federal Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (15 U.S.C. § 7001 et seq., the "**ESIGN Act**") and substantially similar state laws such as the Uniform Electronic Transactions Act ("**UETA**").

By consenting to this Disclosure, you agree that your electronic signature on documents presented in the Service has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature on paper.

---

## 1. Consent to Use Electronic Records and Signatures

When your daycare or employer uses ComplianceKit to collect your signature on a form, consent, acknowledgment, or other compliance document, the document is presented to you electronically and you are asked to sign electronically (for example, by drawing a signature, typing your name, or clicking a button that adopts a signature).

If you consent, the electronic document and your electronic signature will have the same legal effect as a signed paper document. We may maintain the electronic record as the sole official record of the transaction.

---

## 2. Your Right to Receive a Paper Copy

Even if you choose to sign electronically, you have the right to receive a paper copy of any document you sign electronically. To request a paper copy:

- Contact the daycare or employer that asked you to sign. They are the primary recipient of the signed document.
- Alternatively, email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}} with the subject line "Paper Copy Request" and include the document name or date. We will provide a paper copy at no additional charge by mailing it to the address you provide.

Requesting a paper copy does not invalidate a signature you already provided electronically unless you withdraw consent as described in Section 3.

---

## 3. Your Right to Withdraw Consent

You may withdraw consent to use electronic records and signatures at any time before you sign. To withdraw consent:

- Click "Cancel" or close the signing window before completing your signature.
- Notify the daycare or employer that requested your signature.
- Alternatively, email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}} with the subject line "Withdraw E-Signature Consent."

Withdrawing consent means you must complete the transaction by signing a paper copy. Withdrawal does not affect the legal validity of electronic signatures you have already provided in connection with completed transactions.

There is no fee for withdrawing consent.

---

## 4. Hardware and Software Requirements

To sign and access electronic records through the Service, you need:

- A **modern web browser** (current or immediately previous major version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, or a comparable engine).
- A device capable of running a modern browser (desktop, laptop, tablet, or modern smartphone).
- **Internet access** sufficient to load web pages and upload a document.
- An **email address** for verification and record delivery.
- The ability to **view, print, or save PDF documents**.
- For signatures drawn with a finger or stylus, a **touch-capable device** or a mouse/trackpad.

If the requirements change in a way that would limit your ability to access or retain electronic records, we will give you notice and, if required by law, re-request your consent.

---

## 5. Email Address and Contact Updates

We will send communications related to electronic records to the email address on file for your account or, where applicable, to the email address provided by the daycare.

**You are responsible for keeping your email address current.** To update your email address:

- If you have a ComplianceKit account, update it in your account settings.
- If you are signing through a daycare portal and do not have an account, notify the daycare or email {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}} with the subject line "Update Contact Info."

Failing to keep your email current may result in missed notices and delivery of records.

---

## 6. What Gets Captured

When you sign electronically, we capture an audit trail that may include:

- A unique signature ID.
- The time and date of signing (UTC).
- The IP address and browser/device user agent you used.
- An approximate geographic location derived from your IP address (city or country level).
- Verification of the email address (and phone number, if applicable) associated with the signature.
- The cryptographic hash of the document before and after signing (to prove the document was not altered).
- Your declared name and title at signing.
- The version of this Disclosure you consented to.

This information is stored securely and is used to prove the validity of your signature if challenged. Full details are in the [Signature Audit Trail Schema](./signature-audit-trail-schema.md) and governed by our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md).

---

## 7. Record Retention

Electronic signature records are retained for at least **7 years** after signing, to meet regulatory and evidentiary requirements. You may request a copy at any time during that period.

---

## 8. Legal Effect

By providing your electronic signature, you affirm:

- You are the person identified in the signature block (or are the authorized representative of such person).
- You intend to sign the document.
- You have reviewed the document before signing.
- Your electronic signature is your legal signature and has the same effect as if you had signed in ink.
