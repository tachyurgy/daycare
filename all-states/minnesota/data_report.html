6.Violations & Inspection Data

Minnesota runs a robust public-transparency regime. Monitoring-visit results are posted to the Licensing Information Lookup three days after each visit and remain public for four years. Correction orders, licensing actions (suspension, conditional, revocation), and maltreatment investigative memoranda are all posted.

Full details: violations.md

7.Outreach Signals

Regulatory pain points

  • NETStudy 2.0 background studies renewed every 5 years — compliance tracking pain.
  • Annual unannounced inspection + four "Early and Often" visits in year one.
  • Written Risk Reduction Plan must be updated annually + after any significant incident.
  • Shaken Baby / AHT training required annually for staff working with children under 5.
  • Public LIL transparency = reputational pressure to correct fast.

Current events & political heat

  • January 2026 viral video reopened scrutiny of DHS/DCYF child-care fraud controls.
  • Legislative Auditor recommended replacing handwritten sign-in sheets with electronic attendance — product opportunity.
  • 62+ active investigations involving federally-funded MN child care centers (late 2025 / early 2026).
  • Parent Aware QRIS participation is the key "quality-tier" marker for subsidy bonuses.
