7Violations & Inspection Data

Access mode

Per-facility ASP   No enforcement roster

Non-compliance history is exposed on the `get_details.asp` detail page for each facility. There is no single enforcement-actions PDF published on a regular cadence — enforcement is announced via ad-hoc press releases.

Inspection cadence

Unannounced inspections anytime; fire marshal inspection periodic; OEHS food/sanitation annual. Enforcement tools include provisional license, conditions of operation, capacity reduction, emergency suspension (for immediate danger to children).

Key fields exposed per facility

  • Provider name, number, license type, status, expiration
  • Capacity, age range, hours, days
  • Address, city, county, ZIP
  • Licensee / Administrator name and email
  • Non-compliance history (per inspection date, rule section)

Scraping strategy

  1. Seed provider numbers from the 2021 Chart of Open Providers PDF (captured in west_virginia_leads.csv).
  2. Fetch get_details.asp?q={provider_number} for each provider; parse HTML with BeautifulSoup.
  3. Augment with ecewvisearch.asp name/city lookups to discover new entrants since 2021.
  4. Set up RSS/polling against dhhr.wv.gov/News/{YYYY}/Pages/ for enforcement press releases.
  5. File a FOIA for the current 2026 Chart of Open Providers in Excel format.

FOIA path

WV FOIA (W. Va. Code §29B-1-1 et seq.). Custodian: DoHS Bureau for Family Assistance. 5 business day statutory response. Request both (a) current provider roster with email/phone in Excel and (b) full non-compliance history 2015-2026 in CSV.

Sample enforcement action

DoHS press release (Oct 27, 2024) placed Cross Lanes Child Care and Learning Center on provisional license with zero capacity for physical/psychological punishment of children, failure to report serious occurrences, inadequate staff supervision. Process: investigation by BSS+BFA → provisional license with capacity reduction → 30-day appeal window → hearing.

8Outreach Signals

  • Rare email-dense dataset — ~92% email fill from Chart of Open Providers PDF. Email campaigns viable without enrichment.
  • Phone coverage 0% — not in the 2021 PDF; call campaigns require enrichment or per-facility `get_details.asp` parsing.
  • 2024 reorg confusion — DHHR → DoHS transition creates URL churn and compliance questions. Sales angle: "single source of truth across agency changes."
  • Renewal is up to 2 years (longer than most states) — stretches inspection preparation over more time; subscription retention advantage.
  • Water-activity ratios (Appendix 78-1 E Table B) are uniquely strict — ComplianceKit water-safety checklist is a WV-specific selling point.
  • Evening/nighttime care hourly-check rule is unique — niche product differentiator for overnight programs.
  • TEAM for WV Children's "Mapping the Gap" dashboard confirms supply-side scarcity — market is consolidating.
